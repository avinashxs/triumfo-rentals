InfoComm 2026 Las Vegas

InfoComm 2026 is a premier audiovisual trade show in North America. It showcases countless products related to audio, communication and collaboration, video, digital signage, security, AR, VR, and many live events. You can explore new and innovative AV solutions to grow your business and network.

At this conference, more than 100 sessions will be divided into 11 educational tracks, such as AI, audio, broadcast AV, business & project management, conferencing & collaboration, design & integration, enterprise IT, digital signage, events & entertainment, diversity & accessibility, learning spaces, and sustainability.

InfoComm Education and Exhibits 2026 will be held from 13 to 19 June 2026 at the Las Vegas Convention Center, Las Vegas, NV, USA.

Notable Features of the InfoComm Trade Show

InfoComm’s uniqueness as a pro AV trade show lies in the following features.

  • More than 800+ exhibitors will showcase their latest solutions to 30,000 visitors to build lasting relationships.
  • 250+ speakers conduct educational sessions over diverse yet relevant topics and share their valuable knowledge.
  • A platform to learn about the trends and developments in the industry, along with technical courses to improve your career.
  • Opportunity to connect with leaders from the renowned brands in the industry, make connections, and close business deals.

Why Exhibit at the InfoComm 2026 in Las Vegas?

Here are some remarkable advantages of exhibiting at InfoComm in Las Vegas.

  • Showcase the latest audiovisual technologies, products, and services to the end-users from around the world.
  • Gain knowledge about industry trends and practices, along with learning about end-user behaviour.
  • Meet with the professionals from the audiovisual industry worldwide, and discover new businesses to forge partnerships with.
  • Experts will also address the challenges that the industry is facing, along with sharing valuable insights about market dynamics.

Attendees will come from all 50 states of America to InfoComm 2026, looking for new AV solutions. Make a distinguished brand impression on them with a spectacular exhibit.
