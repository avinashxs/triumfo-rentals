Money20/20 2025

Money20/20 Las Vegas 2025 is the largest and most influential trade show which is also known as an event Where Money Does Business. You can attend the largest global fintech event from 26-29 October 2025 in Las Vegas.

In simple words, Money20/20 is an event that focuses on all about the world of money including digital payments, banks, fintech, policies, financial services, start-ups, retail, and much more. Be a part of the fintech world, by exhibiting with exhibit design companies in Las Vegas.

Why Attend Money20/20 Las Vegas 2025?

Money 20/20 will be attended by more than 13000 attendees, and 3000 leading companies of which 7 out of every 10 companies are decision makers. Attending the show will also help you to get 60+ hours of critical content that help you to boost your fintech business.

  • The event will boost up the entire money ecosystem including crypto to start-ups and baking and payment companies.
  • The event will be attended by senior leaders and decision-makers from the fintech community so meeting them will help you to hear them directly.
  • The event will provide the highest visibility to your products and services in front of industry visionaries and other influencing professionals.
  • Meet directly with 1000 professionals from top companies, start-ups, fintech payment companies, and other government authorities.
  • The event will offer live experiences to its attendees and help them get the highest visibility in the international markets.

Exhibitors can schedule countless in-person meetings and can take their business 5 years ahead of the competition. The event will also help you to get the highest ROI and generate leads for the pipeline.
