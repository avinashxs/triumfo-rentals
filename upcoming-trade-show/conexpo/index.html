CONEXPO-CON/AGG 2026 Las Vegas

CONEXPO-CON/AGG 2026 is North America’s largest construction trade show. This expo emphasizes the construction equipment and construction material industries. It offers the opportunity to take your construction business to the next level because here you can find equipment, services, and people within your field.

More than 2000 exhibitors are going to be present at the upcoming edition of this expo in Las Vegas in 2026. They will showcase the latest equipment, products, services, innovations, and technologies related to the construction industry. At this expo, you will meet equipment manufacturers, technical experts, and industry leaders.

The next edition of the CONEXPO-CON/AGG 2026 will be held from March 3 to 7, 2026, at the Los Angeles Convention Center in Los Angeles, CA.

Why Participate in the CONEXPO 2026 in Las Vegas ?

A few notable benefits of participating in the CONEXPO 2026 are mentioned below.

  • You can test the latest equipment and get live demonstrations of the tools you will be using.
  • You can connect with the equipment manufacturers, ask about the products, and get demonstrations from them.
  • Compare many pieces of equipment from different brands to make the best decision for your business.
  • Meet industry experts and technical experts. You can also ask relevant questions and gain insights that can drive your future.
  • Features 190 educational sessions by experts on various construction-related topics.
