Move America 2025 Detroit

MOVE America 2025 is the leading exhibition for mobility tech and start-up companies. It brings together the entire mobility ecosystem under one roof and focuses on business models, technology, and sustainability. It nationwide. The product categories at this trade fair are autonomous vehicle technology, traffic and parking technology, bus technology, active mobility solutions, automotive supply chain, city freight solutions, metro rail technology, infrastructure, design and engineering, batteries and storage, GIS, data and mapping, etc.

Move America 2025 will be held from 24 to 25 September 2025 in Huntington Place, Detroit, MI.

Significant Highlights of Move America

Let’s break down why Move America is a crucial exhibition for professionals across the mobility value chain.

  • The event features 4000+ attendees, 250 exhibitors, 500+ speakers, 200 start-ups, and 1500+ CEOs and high-level professionals.
  • Focuses on innovative, eco-friendly, and sustainable urban transport to save our ecosystem from carbon emissions.
  • Attracts thousands of mobility giants and transport tech companies, offering a unique opportunity to meet the entire industry in one place.
  • An international platform to discuss ideas, build networks, and grow partnerships to propel your business to the next level of success.
