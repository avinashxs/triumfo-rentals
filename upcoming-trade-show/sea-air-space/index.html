Sea Air Space 2026

Sea-Air-Space 2026 is one of the biggest maritime trade shows in the USA. The event will bring the entire key military decision-makers into one place.

There will be more than 15000 participants and 357+ exhibitors on the show floor. Interested visitors can come to the show from April 19 to 22, 2026, at Gaylord National Resort & Convention Center, National Harbor, Maryland.

Key Insights about Sea-Air-Space 2026:

This is the leading maritime exposition organized by the Navy League of the United States. The visitor's profile will include members of the US military, workers in provincial, state, and federal governments in the United States, Members of Congress, professionals from the military and administration, and Navy League contributors. Hence, showcasing the products to them could be beneficial for your business.

If you also want to create brand awareness, then you can exhibit with a trade show exhibit booth in Maryland.

Why Participate in Sea-Air-Space 2026 in Maryland?

  • At professional development discussions, exhibitors will have a chance to showcase the most updated products to high-level attendees.
  • You will get solutions to the most pressing issues of the military industry that will take your business forward. 
  • The upcoming expo will offer countless business-generating prospects to attendees where they can make deals with purchasing agents and policymakers. 
  • The expo offers professional development opportunities to exhibitors as they will get the latest information about the defense sector.
  • There will be guided VIP tours which will help in connecting customers with the suppliers.
