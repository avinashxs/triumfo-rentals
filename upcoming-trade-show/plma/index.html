PLMA Chicago 2025

The Private Label Manufacturers Association (PLMA) Annual Private Label Trade Show is an exceptional event for the Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) industry. This unique event empowers the retail sector. It brings together key players from the private-label manufacturing and retail industries, including food and beverages, household products, personal care, beauty, kitchenware, and more.

PLMA’s 2025 Annual Private Label Trade Show will be held from 16 to 18 November 2025 at the Donald E. Stephens Convention Center in Chicago, USA.

Remarkable Features of the PLMA Annual Private Label Show

Some notable features that make PLMA’s Annual Private Label Trade Show a must-attend event for retail industry professionals are mentioned below.

  • 1,800+ exhibitors from more than 60 countries, including leading retailers, wholesalers, distributors, and industry professionals, participate in this trade show.
  • The event offers a unique platform for businesses to network, learn about emerging trends, and strike valuable deals.
  • From gourmet foods to eco-friendly cleaning solutions, the show will feature a diverse range of private-label products.
  • Highlight the latest advancements in packaging, flavors, and sustainable practices, shaping the future of private label excellence.

Why participate in the PLMA Private Label Trade Show 2025 in Chicago?

Exhibiting at the Private Label Manufacturers Association(PMLA) Annual Private Label Trade Show 2025 in Chicago can be beneficial for your business in many ways. A few of them are mentioned below.

  • Showcase the latest innovations and technologies related to the CPG industry to thousands of interested visitors.
  • Explore new opportunities, strengthen collaborations, and spark innovation within the private label industry.
  • Gain visibility in the international market and network with top-tier retailers, wholesalers, and industry experts.
  • Acquire a competitive advantage, improved consumer loyalty, and an agile response to changing shopper needs.
