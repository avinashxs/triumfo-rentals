IPPE 2026

IPPE Atlanta 2026 stands for the International Production & Processing Expo, a grand gathering where the global poultry, meat, and feed industries come together. It will take place in Atlanta, United States from 27 Jan 2026 to 29 Jan 2026.

IPPE Atlanta 2026 simply aims to empower attendees to thrive in an ever-evolving market landscape through networking, education, and exposure to cutting-edge solutions.

From farm to fork, this expo covers every aspect of production and processing within these industries. Farmers, producers, processors, suppliers, distributors, and enthusiasts from every corner of the globe will arrive at this event.

Attendees can explore a vast array of products like state-of-the-art processing equipment, sustainable feed solutions, and products designed to optimize efficiency, enhance quality, and promote animal welfare.

In the previous edition, over 1,200 exhibitors showcased their latest trends and products among huge potential buyers.

Take various benefits of participating at IPPE Atlanta 2026

  • Collaborate with leading farmers, producers, processors, suppliers, distributors, and enthusiasts to expand your company effectively.
  • Over 30,000 visitors arrived in the previous edition, which helped numerous exhibitors achieve the highest visibility and attain maximum goals.
  • Industry leaders will highlight insights into state-of-the-art processing equipment, sustainable feed solutions, products designed to optimize efficiency, etc.
