SHRM Annual Conference 2026 Orlando

SHRM Annual Conference 2026 is considered the largest exhibition and conference for HR professionals. Here, SHRM stands for the Society for Human Resource Management, and this conference is an acclaimed platform for networking with top business and HR experts. It attracts HR professionals seeking professional development and who want to get ahead in their careers.

During the conference, you can join seminars and hands-on workshops on relevant topics for HR professionals like mental health, AI in the workplace, DE&I, and so much more. At this annual conference, you can gain valuable insights and discuss challenges regarding workplace culture, talent acquisition, workplace issues, and resolutions using the latest HR technologies.

The next edition of the SHRM Annual Conference 2026 will be held from June 16 to 19, 2026, at the Orange County Convention Center, Orlando, USA.

Why participate in the SHRM Annual Conference 2026 in Orlando?

A few of the incredible advantages of this SHRM Conference are mentioned below.

  • It is an event that attracts 15,000+ attendees and offers the opportunity to have face-to-face and online meetings.
  • It features 375+ sessions offering valuable insights on AI advancements, emerging regulations, and the latest best practices in the field.
  • More than 650 exhibitor companies showcase HR products and services, such as recruiting technology, rewards programs, wellness measures, and more.
  • It is a brilliant platform to showcase your products and solutions related to business acumen, strategy, employee relations, retention, technology, rewards, and much more.
  • Meet and connect with HR decision-makers and major executives from across various industries.

The exhibitors at the SHRM Annual Conference 2026 will showcase products and services for all segments of the human resources profession. Make a distinguished brand impression with a professional trade show booth in Orlando by xs-worldwide.
