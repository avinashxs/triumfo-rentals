Top 5 Apparel & Clothing Trade Shows in the USA

In the fashion world, first impressions matter—and there's no better place to make yours than at a leading trade show.

The United States hosts the most significant and impactful global apparel and clothing trade shows. These events are the ultimate platforms to showcase creativity, network with industry leaders, and explore cutting-edge trends. For exhibitors, standing out is crucial, and a strategically designed booth—whether custom-made or a rental booth in the USA—can set the stage for success.

Below, we explore the top five apparel and clothing trade shows in the USA and share insights on making your presence unforgettable.

1. New York Fashion Market Week

Location New York City, New York
Date February 6 -11, 2025
Focus High-end fashion, luxury apparel, and accessories.
Exhibitor Tip Use a sleek, modern trade show booth design to align with the upscale audience.

Known as a luxury and high-end fashion hub, New York Fashion Market Week offers a sophisticated platform for designers and brands.

This event connects brands with elite buyers and influencers, making it an excellent opportunity to elevate your brand's visibility.

2. Texworld USA

Location New York City, New York
Date July 23–25, 2025
Focus Textiles, fabrics, and sustainable materials.
Exhibitor Tip Highlight your product’s innovation with a custom trade show booth featuring interactive displays.

Texworld USA is the go-to trade show for fabric sourcing, innovative materials, and sustainable practices.

This event is ideal for brands focused on sustainability and innovation, making it a hotspot for eco-conscious buyers and manufacturers.

3. Offprice Show

Location Venetian Expo Center, Las Vegas, Nevada
Date August 17–20, 2025
Focus Discounted apparel, accessories, and footwear.
Exhibitor Tip Use bright, attention-grabbing trade show booth designs to emphasize competitive pricing.

The Office Show is a unique trade show where buyers can source discounted products without compromising quality.

This show is perfect for businesses focused on value-driven products, connecting them with a wide range of budget-conscious buyers.

4. MAGIC Las Vegas

Location Las Vegas, Nevada
Date August 18-20, 2025
Focus Men's, women's, and children's apparel, footwear, and accessories.

MAGIC Las Vegas is the crown jewel of the apparel trade show calendar. Drawing exhibitors and attendees from across the globe, it caters to various market segments, from ready-to-wear to accessories.

MAGIC Las Vegas provides unmatched networking opportunities and a chance to showcase your brand to a diverse audience of buyers and retailers.

5. The Atlanta Apparel Show

Location AmericasMart, Atlanta, Georgia
Date October 14–17, 2025
Focus A wide range of apparel categories, including special occasions and children's wear.
Exhibitor Tip Opt for a trade show exhibition rental in the USA for consistent branding across multiple events.

As a year-round destination for wholesale buyers, the Atlanta Apparel Show offers diverse collections, including bridal wear, casual attire, and more.

This event's frequency provides buyers with continuous exposure and the opportunity to adapt your offerings throughout the year.

Maximizing Trade Show Success

A stunning trade show booth is the key to standing out. Whether you’re investing in a booth or opting for an exhibition rental in the USA, consider these tips:

  • Plan Strategically: Reserve your booth space early and design it to suit the event's audience.
  • Leverage Technology: Incorporate digital displays, virtual product showcases, or AR experiences for added engagement.
  • Emphasise Branding: Ensure your booth's design reflects your brand's identity through color schemes, signage, and layouts.
  • Engage Visitors: Have knowledgeable staff to interact with attendees and demonstrate products.
