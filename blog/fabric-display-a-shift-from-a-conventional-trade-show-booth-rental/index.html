Everything You Need to Know About Fabric Displays

With changing times, the ways of promoting your brand in trade shows are evolving at a significant pace. Now people not only get attracted to a custom-made booth or a traditional booth but also pay significant attention to unconventional trade show booth styles. Such a trend has encouraged exhibitors to shift their focus and embrace fabric displays.

Let’s take a glance at what fabric displays are all about, how many types of fabric displays are there, and how they can be beneficial for your brand.

What is Fabric Display?

Fabric displays are fabric-based trade show displays that are generally used in commercial or public spaces. They are ideal for trade shows, sports arenas, retail stores, and many such places where you want to grab the attention of your audience towards your brand. Also, they are available in an affordable range.

They generally give a very distinctive look and make your trade show booth rental stand out from others by making it more interactive and flexible. These types of displays generally use dye-sublimation technology to make your fabric wrinkle-free, thereby helping to draw the attention of potential customers.

What are the major types of Fabric Displays?

Let us have a sneak peek into the 4 most popular types of fabric displays.

1. Pop-up Fabric Displays

Pop-up fabric displays come in a collapsible frame, which makes them easy to carry from one trade show to another. Its collapsible property gives it the ability to combine the frame. Also, their structured nature relieves you from the tension of assembling and reassembling graphics.

2. Hanging Fabric Displays

As the name “hanging fabric displays” shows, these display booths are generally hung up from the ceiling instead of placed on the floor like the other tradeshow booth rentals. Hanging signs offer the best way to get the attention of the attendees across the exhibit hall.

3. Backlit Fabric Displays

Fabric displays are the most creative way of grabbing the attention of attendees, and if we add backlit to normal fabric displays then it will help in positioning a crystal-clear brand message within the minds of attendees. Backlit fabric displays are the ones in which we add the lights on the backdrop and then a standard display into a brighter and more attractive backdrop.

4. Tabletop Fabric Displays

Tabletop fabric displays are easy to manage and transport display booths. These are generally more compact than the ones hanging from a wall or ceiling, and they can comfortably fit on the top of the table. They have a very lightweight frame that can easily hold the graphics. Due to their simplicity and uniqueness, it is quite a popular choice among the trade show’s participants.

These are some of the fabric displays that can be chosen based on your brand identity and the message you want to convey to your audience.

What are the benefits of Fabric Displays?

Let’s see a few of the prominent benefits that fabric displays offer.

● Hassle-free assembly

Installations of all the trade show booth displays are not the same, so in the case of fabric displays. Due to their collapsible frame, these displays can be assembled within a short time and can help you get ready to amaze your audience within no time.

● Reduces transit cost

If we talk about custom-built booths or any other traditional booths, then due to their heavy weight, their handling and shipping will be costly. On the other hand, if we talk about fabric displays, which are also known as pop-up and portable displays, are considered lightweight and easy to transport. These traits reduce the unnecessary transit cost. 

● Wrinkle-free appearance

Fabric displays banish the chances of unprofessional or wrinkled graphics in your trade show booth. These types of displays offer wrinkle-free, high-quality appearance. It aids in the proper maintenance of your graphics work.
