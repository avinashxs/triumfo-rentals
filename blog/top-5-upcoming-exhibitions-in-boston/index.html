Top 5 Upcoming Trade Shows in Boston 2025-2026

Boston is an amazing place for tourists! However, it also has another brilliant side, the business side. This marvelous city hosts numerous trade shows that help exhibit companies in multiple ways, such as increasing sales, enhancing brand awareness, boosting growth, and much more.

If you are also planning to expand your business, participate in the appropriate trade shows. First, choose the right events, then contact the best exhibit design companies in Boston for an engaging and interactive trade show exhibit booth to showcase your brand in style.

Let us take a look at five leading trade shows in Boston that you can consider participating in to help grow your business.

1. Harpoon Octoberfest 2025

Dates 03 - 04 October 2025
Venue Harpoon Brewery, Boston, MA, USA
Focus Beer community

The Harpoon Oktoberfest provides a leading platform for professionals in the beer industry to connect with peers and grow their professional network. Various industry leaders, leading companies, experts, and decision-makers attend this fest.

It features many fun activities, like live oompah music, pretzel-eating contests, stein-hoisting competitions, chicken dancing, and more. Tens of thousands of beer lovers and hundreds of exhibitors from different levels of the industry participate in this unique beer event. It means you get unparalleled opportunities to attract numerous potential buyers.

2. Seafood Expo North America 2026

Dates 15 - 17 March 2026
Venue Boston Convention & Exhibition Center, Boston, MA, USA
Focus Seafood processing and supply chain

The Seafood Expo North America (Boston) is a leading trade show for the Food and Beverage industry dedicated to seafood processing and supply chain. It brings together diverse seafood buyers and processors, such as catering, food service, retail, and restaurant, under one roof.

More than 22,500 visitors and 1,300+ exhibitors mark their presence in this seafood-based event. Participate in this international event to explore suppliers, products, and trends while making meaningful business relationships. Achieve your sales and business goals through this food expo and get ahead of your competition.

3. New England Restaurant & Bar Show 2026

Dates 26 - 27 April 2026
Venue Boston Convention & Exhibition Center, Boston, MA, USA
Focus Restaurant, bar, and food service

The New England Restaurant and Bar Show is a reputed event in the food service, restaurant, and bar industry. It showcases the latest services, products, and technologies. It features a comprehensive education program and innovative demos. These programs provide restaurants, retail, and food service providers with valuable insights to optimize their business practices.

This show features 300+ exhibitors and brands. As an exhibitor, you can showcase your food products, services, culinary demos, and education sessions to the best food service buyers. You can also learn about the latest technology, education, culinary trends, and more here. Stay up-to-date in a constantly developing industry and gain a competitive edge for your business.

4. PEGS Boston Summit 2026

Dates 11 - 15 May 2026
Venue Omni Boston Hotel at the Seaport, Boston, MA, USA
Focus Essential protein and antibody engineering

PEGS Conference and Expo is a distinguished event for protein & antibody engineering research professionals. It attracts eminent experts and industry leaders associated with biologics and protein engineering. This summit is a platform to share the latest developments in drug development, protein and antibody engineering, radiotherapy, immunotherapy, and biologics.

If you are working on Protein & Antibody Engineering Research and want to share or learn more about a variety of topics, the PEGS Summit Boston is the perfect place for you. Gain unparalleled insights into specialized topics from experts. Acquire practical skills and knowledge through interactive learning and live sessions.

5. Bio-IT World Conference & Expo 2026

Dates 19 - 21 May 2026
Venue Omni Boston Hotel at the Seaport, Boston, MA, USA
Focus Innovations in advanced precision medicine

Bio-IT World Expo and Conference is a distinct event that talks about technologies and analytic approaches that drive the future of precision medicine. It brings 2,800+ eminent professionals with diverse expertise together in one place. Experts in life sciences, clinical research, pharmaceuticals, healthcare, informatics, and technology come together to solve problems and accelerate science related to advanced precision medicine.

Bio-IT World Conference program features keynotes and 200+ educational and technical presentations. You can attend technical workshops, interactive panels, and hands-on symposia here. It has an exhibit hall to showcase cutting-edge technologies and solutions. As an exhibitor, you get incredible networking and learning opportunities to transform your business.

Proven Tips to Achieve Success at Trade Shows in Boston

Know Your Audience: Research the regional demographics and attendee profile for better engagement. Craft your messaging and trade show strategies around them.

Design a Compact Yet Striking Booth: Boston venues can be competitive. However, you can stand out with a compact 10x20 booth with sleek visuals and concise messaging.

Network Strategically: Boston trade shows draw top-tier professionals worldwide. Leverage that advantage to expand your network. Set meetings with them in advance.

Offer Value: Provide demos, samples, or informative content to capture interest.

Follow Up Promptly: Nurture leads immediately post-event to boost trade show ROI.
