Trade Show Marketing Strategy

Marketing strategies play a major role in the success of a business. Marketing is not something that is very new. In fact, marketing strategies have been adopted for a very long time to promote business and trade. With the coming up of technology and social media, the methods of how marketing is approached have certainly been affected. But the essence remains the same.

With the way trade shows have shown remarkable success in promoting businesses, they are considered to be an important aspect of marketing strategy for big and small organizations. Not just the products and services, trade shows are also the medium for marketing new ideas and discussions that can reach the masses in a short span of time. In ancient times simple strategies were practiced to showcase products and services. But now the practice has taken up a much more extensive face. New technological means have taken up the place of simple showcasing techniques. The trade show booth builders play an important role in these times while promoting the products and services. The booth designs and features that the trade show booth builders provide are such that they can make the heart skip a beat. They are a very good technique to gather traffic at the booth. Whatever the talent of the staff who is showcasing the products and services, the idea of the trade show is incomplete without mesmerizing trade show booth ideas nowadays.

If the question arises about how we define trade show marketing strategy then we can define it in the following way.

In the answer to the above question, we can say that we can define trade show marketing strategy as a technique where we participate in events such that we can increase our ROI by showcasing products and services and by exchanging dialogues with consumers, rivals, and people from media. Whether it is accomplished via fantastic trade show booth ideas or through the expertise of the trained staff appointed on the exhibition floor, the main motive of exhibiting is to reach out to a wide audience and increase the ROI from the show.  

There are many ideas that can be practiced to increase the ROI of the industry at trade shows. We shall discuss some of the ideas in this blog.

Define a goal

Prior to reaching the trade show, it is an important practice to define your goal and objective from a trade show. What do you want to achieve through the trade show? Are you looking to introduce new product launches or you are targeting the existing product with additional features that can benefit the customer in some way better than the earlier one? Or you are competing with your rivals and want your product to be seen? In all cases when you define your goal, the idea becomes clear as to how you shall go about your trade show and what staff and type of booth you would be needing. A trade show booth builder can really be of much help in setting the right environment for your trade show.

Spend on your exhibition stand

Once you have figured out what is that you want to achieve with your trade show, the next step is to find the right trade show booth ideas that can help you get more traffic to the booth. There are several methods and designs nowadays that can help you achieve your objective. Many people find the use of technology like virtual walls, and interactive floors a good option. While others use giveaways as a means to attract more traffic to the booth. Whatever the way, your exhibition stand must be your primary means to get you more traffic so spend on it wisely.

Pre-trade show marketing and Follow-up

According to the research, around 75 percent of people who turn up at trade shows use pre-trade show information to plan their agenda. Therefore it is important to invest in pre-trade show marketing as people need time to think and plan for the trade shows. Social media is a big help in marketing trade shows. Websites and social media platforms together make good resources for posting updates about trade shows. They are even helpful in posting blogs and other articles post the trade show and are an essential medium nowadays for follow-up after the trade shows. It is said that the actual business actually starts after the trade show is over because whatever sales have been possible in the trade show is one thing and making your brand known amongst people from across the world is another. People will know your brand after the trade show is over and you are likely to be approached by potential customers where you have been able to make a mark. Many exhibitors use custom trade show exhibits made according to their specifications to create a big impact on the trade show visitors such that they are likely to be remembered after the trade show is over.

Trade show preparation

Trade show marketing strategy requires you to make your trade show preparation well ahead of time. Plan for your logistics and necessary equipment before the trade show to avoid miscalculations. Keep enough marketing collateral so that you are able to connect with the visitors on all levels of needs and are able to provide them when they ask for it.

Arrange for the trained staff

No matter how wonderful your trade show booth is, you still need trained staff on the exhibition floor to make your event successful. Think in terms of the visitors and choose the people that are outgoing and have the ability to keep the audience engaged. Trained staff with pleasing mannerisms and an efficient salesperson is a trade show’s major requirement from the staff being appointed for the trade show. If they are able to connect with the audience and able to convince them of your products and services then your ROI is bound to shoot up and you can expect to have a successful event.
