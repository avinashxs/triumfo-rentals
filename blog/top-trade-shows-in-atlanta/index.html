Top 10 Trade Shows in Atlanta

Atlanta is one of the largest cities not only in the US but in the entire world. It claims to have the third-largest concentration of Fortune 500 companies. This global city has impeccable opportunities for business across all the major sectors. You can secure those opportunities through participating in trade shows, exhibitions, and conventions organized in this city.

You just need to determine the right trade event for your business and showcase it distinctively with a smartly crafted trade show booth in Atlanta. You can kickstart your trade show journey right here by exploring some of the most prominent trade fairs scheduled there in the upcoming days.

Here are the top ten trade shows in Atlanta that you can consider participating in.

1. Atlanta (Winter) Market 2026

Date: 13 - 19 January 2026
Location: AmericasMart Atlanta, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Wholesale sourcing destination for a broad selection of products

Atlanta Winter Market is a unique marketplace for home furnishings, gifts, and design trades. This event attracts buyers, retailers, interior designers, architects, and purchasing companies. It features diversified educational seminars, networking events, and other amenities. This marketplace is a renowned platform for cross-category shopping and networking.

More than 6,000+ brands exhibit their latest home décor, furnishings, and apparel items at this trade show. As a dynamic wholesale sourcing destination, this is an excellent event to discover new products, ideas, and vendors for your business. If you are a business owner, manufacturer, sales associate, or buyer, this is a must-attend event for you.

2. International Production & Processing Expo (IPPE) 2026

Date: 27 - 29 January 2026
Location: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Poultry and egg, meat and animal food

The International Production and Processing Expo (IPPE) is one of the largest events for the poultry and egg, meat, and animal food industry. It attracts industry leaders worldwide and focuses on business development, optimizing practices and operations to stay competitive. This event allows you to connect and build relationships with new suppliers, vendors, and peers in the industry.

IPPE 2026 brings the global audience of meat, poultry, egg, and animal food professionals under one roof. They visit this event to explore the latest solutions, technologies, research, processes, services, and products across the animal food and protein industries. If you deal in supplies, services, technology, or equipment for the production and processing of eggs, meat, and poultry, you must participate in this event.

3. Dallas Safari Club (DSC) Convention & Sporting Expo 2026

Date: 06 - 08 February 2026
Location: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Conservation and education about wildlife and related resources

The DSC Convention & Sporting Expo is one of the most prestigious conservation fundraising events in the US. It supports wildlife conservation, education, and advocacy initiatives for sustainable use of wild resources at the global level. This expo features notable outfitters, gear manufacturers, and conservation partners. It also features extensive banquets and auctions.

If you are interested in wildlife conservation and looking for opportunities for public engagement, education, and advocating for highly regulated hunting, the DSC Convention is the right event for you. Participate and learn valuable insights about shooting and science-based wildlife management.

4. Global Textile Trade Fair (GTTF) 2026

Date: 06 - 08 February 2026
Location: Maison 6405, Duluth, GA, USA
Focus: Apparel, fabrics, and home textiles

The Global Textile Trade Fair is a highly anticipated event for textile professionals around the world. This trade show showcases the latest trends in the industry, from sustainable fabric materials to cutting-edge apparel manufacturing technology. You can explore a wide collection of products under categories like fabrics, apparel & ready to wear, khadi, fibre, silk, wool & woolen, handicraft & handloom, and home textile.

GTTF brings unparalleled opportunities for networking, innovation, and growth for retailers, wholesalers, and importers dealing in textile products. Along with exploring the global trends, you can also connect with eminent experts and industry leaders who are defining the future of the textile industry. Build valuable connections with buyers and suppliers from diverse markets to expand your professional network.

5. VERTICON 2026

Date: 09 - 12 March 2026
Location: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Global vertical aviation industry

VERTICON is a prominent conference and trade show for the global vertical aviation industry. This event showcases the latest products and innovations in helicopters and other vertical-capable aircraft. It showcases 60+ aircraft and features 120+ educational sessions, workshops, and forums on crucial topics and challenges in the industry.

This event brings 600+ industry suppliers as exhibitors and witnesses 15,000 industry professionals as visitors from 75+ countries. You can attend VAI working group meetings and other events to build professional relationships with industry peers. Must participate in this event to learn industry best practices, tools, and tactics across 12 tracks and use them to boost your operational efficiency.

6. GROOM’D 2026

Date: 13 - 15 March 2026
Location: Georgia International Convention Center, College Park, GA, USA
Focus: Pet grooming professionals

The GROOM’D, earlier known as Atlanta Pet Fair & Conference, is a unique trade show that focuses on pet grooming. It attracts pet stylists, pet retailers, pet products manufacturers & suppliers, and more. This pet trade show offers valuable educational resources, technical skills, and the right tools that aid the growth of your pet business.

This pet trade show offers a remarkable platform to connect with the prominent grooming supply vendors. You can showcase your pet grooming talent in competitions at this show. Also, acquire the pet professional education and learn the latest safety best practices to get to the forefront of the modern grooming industry.

7. ACS National Meeting & Exposition Spring 2026

Date: 22 - 26 March 2026
Location: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Scientific and technical knowledge in the field of chemistry

The American Chemical Society (ACS) Meeting and Expo is one of the most important events for chemistry professionals. It attracts thousands of chemistry experts who participate in this event to share advanced scientific and technical knowledge. This expo is a distinguished platform for chemistry enthusiasts looking to explore the latest advancements in the field.

If you are a chemistry professional or student looking for promising opportunities for career growth, professional development, and networking, the ACS Meeting & Exposition is the perfect event for you. Connect with one of the world's largest chemistry-based scientific societies and accelerate your career growth in this dynamic, ever-evolving field.

8. MODEX Show 2026

Date: 13 - 16 April 2026
Location: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Manufacturing and supply chain solutions

The MODEX Show is a premier trade fair for manufacturing and supply chain professionals. It is a renowned platform to showcase the latest equipment and tech solutions for supply chain operations. You can connect with key suppliers through one-on-one meetings. Learn the latest trends through a conference with 4 keynotes and 180+ sessions.

At this trade show, 1,100+ leading solutions providers exhibit cost-cutting equipment and technology solutions that can take your supply chain operations to the next level of proficiency. Therefore, if you are looking for solutions or insights to empower your supply chain business, you can’t miss participating in the MODEX Show.

9. Atlanta Women's Expo Spring 2026

Date: 02 - 03 May 2026
Location: Atlanta Convention Center, Atlanta, USA
Focus: Women’s shopping experience

The Atlanta Women's Expo, or Ultimate Women’s Expo, offers almost everything a woman needs, be it shopping, fashion, food, beauty treatments, haircuts, massages, Celebrity Keynote Speakers, or retail therapy. This expo attracts thousands of buyers seeking the best products & services for personal shopping.

A huge number of local and national brands, companies, and entrepreneurs exhibit here. As an exhibitor, you get an excellent opportunity to present your business to a new and untouched audience who can become your loyal customers. Participate in this expo to connect face-to-face with interested shoppers, get incredible brand visibility, and turn it into sales.

10. International Woodworking Fair (IWF) Atlanta 2026

Date: 25 – 28 August 2026
Location: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Wood products design and technology

The International Woodworking Fair (IWF) is a distinct trade show where you can discover woodworking's latest products, services, and business solutions. This event attracts 26,000+ visitors from across the U.S. and 90 countries worldwide. They come to IWF to explore the unique innovations, ideas, and technologies showcased by top woodworking manufacturers.

IWF welcomes 1,000+ exhibiting companies, including 370+ international exhibitors from 45+ countries, making it North America’s largest global woodworking trade show. Must participate in this trade fair to connect with highly promising buyers, build meaningful relationships, and transform the future of your woodworking-based business.

Expert Tips to Make the Most of Your Participation in Trade Shows in Atlanta

Choose the Right Booth Location: Prioritize high-traffic zones for your trade show booth rental. It can be near entrances or lounges.

Understand Local Preferences: Align your brand messaging with Atlanta’s regional trends.

Use Eye-Catching Booth Design: Invest in clean, engaging, and brand-oriented visuals even if you have a 10x10 booth.

Leverage Pre-Show Marketing: Promote your brand presence on social media and email.

Offer Giveaways or Demos: Engage attendees with free product samples or hands-on experiences.
