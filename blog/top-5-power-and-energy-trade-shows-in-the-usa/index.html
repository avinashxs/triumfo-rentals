Top 5 Power & Energy Trade Shows in the USA 2025-2026

Trade shows and exhibitions offer remarkable opportunities to gain valuable clientele and build potential relationships with eminent professionals in the industry. If you are a decision-maker or an owner of a business in the power and energy sector, attending relevant exhibitions can significantly enhance your commercial growth.

Here are the top 5 power and energy trade shows in the USA scheduled during the upcoming months. Find the most appropriate ones that you can participate in, and partner with an expert trade show booth builder to make the most of it.

1. The Energy Expo

Scheduled Date 20 - 21 August 2025
Location Broward County Convention Center, Fort Lauderdale, FL, USA

The Energy Expo is a premier event connecting professionals in the clean energy, sustainability, and efficiency sectors. It brings together manufacturers, distributors, government agencies, and industry leaders to explore the latest innovations in renewable energy, energy storage, smart technologies, and more. Attendees can discover cutting-edge solutions, network with key decision-makers, and gain insights from expert-led conferences.

Designed for businesses and organizations seeking to expand in the U.S. and Latin American markets, The Energy Expo offers a dynamic platform for collaboration and growth. If you are looking to showcase your products to an interested audience or stay ahead of industry trends, this is a perfect event for you.

2. Oklahoma’s Oil & Natural Gas Expo

Scheduled Date October 9, 2025
Location Bennett Event Center, Oklahoma, USA

Oklahoma’s Oil & Natural Gas Expo is the exclusive event that showcases the latest innovations, technologies, and advancements in the energy sector. Bringing together industry leaders, professionals, and innovators, the expo provides a platform for networking, knowledge-sharing, and business growth. You can explore cutting-edge equipment, attend expert-led discussions, and discover solutions shaping the future of oil and natural gas.

The event highlights the industry’s evolving landscape with a focus on sustainability, efficiency, and emerging trends. Whether you are a seasoned professional or new to the field, the expo offers valuable insights, collaboration opportunities, and access to key decision-makers driving energy forward.

3. Solar & Storage Finance USA

Scheduled Date 21 - 22 October 2025
Location 360 Madison Avenue, New York, USA

Solar & Storage Finance USA is the top event for investors, developers, and financiers associated with renewable energy. The conference brings together the key industry leaders and allows them to explore investment opportunities, market trends, and financial innovations in solar and energy storage. You can gain insights into policy developments, risk management strategies, and emerging business models driving the sector’s growth.

With expert-led discussions, networking sessions, and exclusive deal-making opportunities, the event provides a platform to connect with top-tier stakeholders. If you are looking to expand your portfolio or navigate the evolving landscape of renewable energy, you can’t miss this event.

4. DISTRIBUTECH International

Scheduled Date 02 - 05 February 2026
Location San Diego Convention Center, San Diego, CA, USA

DISTRIBUTECH International, or ‘DTECH’, is the renowned annual event for transmission and distribution professionals. It assembles 20,000 visitors, including the top industry leaders, innovators, and technology providers. It is a dynamic platform for exploring cutting-edge solutions in grid modernization, renewable integration, energy storage, cybersecurity, and more. You gain valuable insights through expert-led sessions, hands-on demonstrations, and networking opportunities with key stakeholders in the energy sector.

The event showcases the latest advancements in power delivery, featuring an extensive exhibition floor with state-of-the-art technologies and services. It provides utilities, engineers, and decision-makers with the knowledge and tools needed to enhance grid reliability, efficiency, and sustainability.

5. RE+ Northeast

Scheduled Date 04 -05 February 2026
Location Boston Convention and Exhibition Center, Boston, USA

RE+ Northeast is the leading clean energy event in the region, connecting industry leaders, innovators, and policymakers to drive the future of renewable energy. Covering solar, energy storage, electrification, and emerging technologies, the event offers insightful discussions, cutting-edge solutions, and valuable networking opportunities.

At this expo, you can explore the latest trends, connect with top companies, and gain expert insights through panel discussions, workshops, and exhibits. If you are a developer, installer, investor, or policymaker, RE+ Northeast provides a distinct platform to collaborate, learn, and shape the future of clean energy in the Northeast and beyond.

Here are expert tips for achieving success at a Power and Energy Trade Show in the USA:

  • Set Clear Objectives: Define specific goals for the event. It may be lead generation, brand awareness, or partnership building.
  • Design an Impactful Booth: Use a modern, open booth layout with interactive displays to attract attention.
  • Showcase Innovations: Highlight cutting-edge energy solutions or sustainable technologies.
  • Engage with Demos: Live product demonstrations help clarify your offering and spark meaningful conversations.
  • Utilise Digital Tools: Use QR codes, apps, or touchscreens for engagement and data capture.
