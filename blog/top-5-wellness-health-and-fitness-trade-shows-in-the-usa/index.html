Top 5 Wellness Health & Fitness Trade Shows in the USA

Healthcare, beauty, and wellness have gained significant popularity among individuals worldwide. If you are a professional or a business in this industry looking to leverage that opportunity for growth, participating in the right business events is essential.

Here are the 5 most prominent health, wellness, beauty, and fitness trade shows and events scheduled in the upcoming months in the USA. If you’re planning to showcase your brand, consider a trade show exhibit booth in the USA to make a lasting impression.

1. International Congress of Esthetics and Spa (ICES) Long Beach 2025

Scheduled Date 24 - 25 August 2025
Location Long Beach Convention Center, Long Beach, CA, USA
Focus Skin care and spa services, and solutions

The International Congress of Esthetics and Spa (ICES) is an outstanding event for beauty, skincare, and wellness professionals. This dynamic trade show brings together industry leaders, educators, and top brands to showcase the latest trends, technologies, and techniques in esthetics and spa treatments. Attendees can explore cutting-edge products, attend expert-led workshops, and gain hands-on experience to elevate their skills.

Designed for estheticians, spa owners, and beauty professionals, ICES Long Beach offers unparalleled networking opportunities and insights into the future of skincare. From live demonstrations to business-boosting strategies, this event is a must for those looking to stay ahead in the industry.

2. International Congress of Esthetics and Spa (ICES) Florida 2025

Scheduled Date 19 - 20 October 2025
Location Broward County Convention Center, Fort Lauderdale, FL, USA
Focus Skin care and spa services, and solutions

The International Congress of Esthetics and Spa is one of the leading events connecting beauty professionals, skincare experts, and wellness innovators from around the world. This dynamic conference offers a platform for industry leaders to showcase the latest trends, cutting-edge technologies, and advanced techniques in esthetics and spa treatments. Attendees will have the opportunity to explore a vast exhibition floor, network with top brands, and gain insights from renowned educators.

With a diverse lineup of workshops, live demonstrations, and expert-led discussions, this event is designed to inspire and empower professionals at every level. Whether you're seeking new products, business growth strategies, or hands-on training, this congress is the ultimate destination for beauty and wellness excellence.

3. Natural Products Expo West 2026

Scheduled Date 03 - 06 March 2026
Location Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Natural and organic products

The Natural Products Expo West is one of the premier trade shows in the natural, organic, and healthy products industry. This event brings together a diverse community of retailers, distributors, manufacturers, and health-conscious consumers. It showcases a wide array of products, including organic foods, clean beauty items, dietary supplements, and eco-friendly home goods.

The NPE (West) also features educational sessions, networking opportunities, and showcases of emerging brands, making it a must-attend event for industry professionals seeking to stay ahead of market trends.

4. International Esthetics, Cosmetics & Spa Conference (IECSC) 2026

Scheduled Date 08 - 10 March 2026
Location Javits Convention Center, New York City, USA
Focus Spa and esthetics trends and solutions

The International Esthetics, Cosmetics & Spa Conference (IECSC) is a premier event for beauty and wellness professionals, bringing together industry leaders, top brands, and innovative thinkers. This must-attend expo offers a dynamic platform to discover the latest advancements in skincare, cosmetics, spa treatments, and wellness technologies. Attendees can explore cutting-edge products, attend expert-led educational sessions, and connect with like-minded professionals to elevate their careers.

With a diverse range of exhibitors and hands-on workshops, IECSC New York provides invaluable insights into emerging trends and business strategies. Whether you're an esthetician, spa owner, or beauty entrepreneur, this event is the perfect opportunity to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry.

5. Total Product Expo 2026

Scheduled Date 31 March - 2 April 2026
Location Las Vegas Convention Center, Las Vegas, NV, USA
Focus Tobacco, cigar, vape, alternative, and general merchandise

The Total Product Expo (TPE) is North America's premier B2B trade show for the tobacco, vapor, CBD, and alternative products industries. TPE attracts over 15,000 attendees and more than 500 exhibitors. The event showcases a diverse range of products, including cigars, e-cigarettes, nicotine pouches, hemp-derived goods, snacks, apparel, and general merchandise.

TPE 2026 offers a dynamic platform for networking, product discovery, and business development, making it a must-attend event for industry professionals.

Expert Suggestions for Successful Participation in Wellness, Health & Fitness Trade Shows

  • Define Clear Objectives: Know what you want to achieve, whether it’s brand awareness, lead generation, or product launch.
  • Design an Attractive Booth: Use clean, calming aesthetics and interactive displays to reflect wellness values and engage attendees.
  • Highlight Benefits Over Features: Showcase how your product or service improves health and lifestyle outcomes.
  • Conduct Live Demonstrations: Let visitors experience your product through demos, sampling, or fitness activities.
  • Engage with Storytelling: Share testimonials and case studies that connect emotionally with your audience.
  • Employ Qualified and Trained Staff: Ensure your booth team is knowledgeable, friendly, and enthusiastic about your offerings.
  • Follow Up Promptly: After the show, reach out to leads with personalised messages and relevant information to convert interest into business.
