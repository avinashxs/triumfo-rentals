Top 5 Upcoming Trade Shows in Dallas in 2025-2026

Dallas is the home of several Fortune 500 companies, like Kimberly-Clark Corporation, ExxonMobil, AT&T, Southwest Airlines, and JCPenney. It has a thriving economy and boasts tremendous financial attributes, such as a high GDP, attractive real estate markets, and a helpful business community.

The city is a great place to start a business and offers excellent opportunities for business owners and entrepreneurs. The trade fairs of Dallas have so much to offer to the exhibitors who want to expand their business operations. To capitalize on these opportunities, you must showcase your brand with the finest trade show exhibit rentals in Dallas.

In this blog, we have listed five important upcoming trade shows in Dallas, making it a must-read for you if you are looking for events worth participating in across the city.

1. Small Business Expo 2025

Date 19 November 2025
Location Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center, Dallas, TX, USA
Focus Solutions and education for success in business

Small Business Expo is America's biggest networking and educational event for entrepreneurs, business owners, and start-up companies. As an exhibitor, you can reach thousands of business owners with strong purchasing power. They attend this event seeking valuable insights to transform the future of their small businesses.

This expo features an extensive business conference, business-critical workshops, and many networking events. It attracts thousands of businesspeople looking for products and services to run a successful business. Participate in this expo to connect with top decision-makers and leverage the energetic and collaborative environment here to build lasting and meaningful relationships with like-minded small business owners.

2. Health and Fitness Expo 2025

Date 12 - 13 December 2025
Location Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center, Dallas, TX, USA
Focus Products and information on running, fitness, and health

The Health and Fitness Expo Dallas is a premier event for running, fitness, and health enthusiasts and associated businesses. Organized by Cumulus Media, this two-day event showcases the finest merchandise, information, and exhibits related to health and wellness. Held along with the BMW Dallas Marathon, it attracts a huge number of fitness enthusiasts.

If you deal in running, fitness, and health solutions, exhibiting at this expo will be very beneficial for your business. Showcase your fitness and wellness products to a highly interested health-conscious audience and not only boost brand awareness but also generate significant sales.

3. Lightovation: Dallas International Lighting Show 2026

Date 10 - 13 January 2026
Location Dallas Market Center, Dallas, TX, USA
Focus Lighting equipment

Lightovation, the Dallas International Lighting Show, is a unique trade event dedicated to lighting solutions. It features a wide array of residential lighting. This trade show attracts a huge number of manufacturers, retailers, and businesses dealing in lighting equipment. They showcase a wide selection of the latest trends and lighting products for indoor and outdoor, decorative and architectural, and smart systems.

Lightovation also offers learning opportunities through educational sessions on a variety of topics such as lighting design, technology, and business strategies. You can gain valuable insights and knowledge that can sharpen your professional skills and optimize your business practices.

4. SDallas Bridal Show & Wedding Expo 2026

Date 07 - 08 February 2026
Location Dallas Market Center, Dallas, TX, USA
Focus Wedding solutions

Dallas Bridal Show is a one-stop shop for everything you need for a perfect wedding. Hundreds of professional wedding businesses and professionals participate in this wedding expo. You can find wedding planners, photographers, wedding cake bakers, florists, jewelers, catering service providers, wedding venue owners, wedding gown & dress designers, and so on.

If you deal in wedding-oriented services and solutions, and want to promote them among engaged couples and their families, you must participate in the Dallas Bridal Show and Wedding Expo. Exhibit at this wedding show to boost your brand visibility among brides and grooms and within the wedding industry.

5. Dallas Travel Show 2026

Date 28 - 29 March 2026
Location Dallas Market Center, Dallas, TX, USA
Focus Tour and travel solutions

The Dallas Travel and Adventure Show is a distinguished travel show. It brings together 250+ holiday destinations, tour companies, cruise lines, and travel service providers under one roof. They come up with exclusive show-only travel deals and specials, allowing travellers and holiday planners to book their dream vacation at unbelievable prices.

This show features in over 60 travel seminars, where you can learn travel tips and advice from the experts. It also offers the opportunity to meet your favorite travel celebrities, including Chris Burkard and Andrew McCarthy. As an exhibitor, you get endless business opportunities as you come across thousands of travel enthusiasts planning their next tour.

Tips for Generating Maximum ROI from Trade Shows in Dallas

Define trade show goals: Set clear goals, such as lead generation, brand awareness, or forming partnerships, and focus on them during trade show planning and execution.

Know your audience: Tailor your brand messaging according to the Dallas market's needs.

Eye-catching booth: Get a custom trade show display with Clear branding, open layout, and engaging visuals.

Train booth staff: Ensure your booth staff are equipped with skills for friendly, knowledgeable, and proactive engagement.
