Top 10 Upcoming Exhibitions in Dubai - UAE

Trade shows are among the best marketing tools for a business entity for many reasons. Every year, professionals and experts from different industries come together at these events to share the latest trends in the market and showcase their products and services to promote their brands. Trade fairs help to keep you updated with the latest upcoming technologies in the world. They have an element of creating cross-cultural awareness at the International level by people coming in from all across the globe.

Trade events in Dubai hold a distinct position in the exhibition world. Being a business hub located at a highly strategic place on the globe, Dubai offers incredible business opportunities to big and small businesses across all the major industries. If you are a frequent exhibitor and haven’t participated in any event here, you are missing a lot.

Let us have a glimpse of the top 10 upcoming exhibitions in Dubai, where you can consider taking part.

1. International Apparel & Textile Fair (IATF) 2025

Dates 17 - 19 November 2025
Venue World Trade Centre Dubai, Dubai, UAE
Category Apparel and Clothing

The International Apparel and Textile Fair is one of the most prominent trade events across the textile industry in the MENA region. It serves as an exceptional platform for sourcing fashion fabrics, garments, handbags, clothing accessories, and footwear. This event offers you incredible networking opportunities across the global fashion industry. Exhibit at the IATF and boost your brand’s reach and reputation in the textile and apparel market.

2. Big 5 Global 2025

Dates 24 - 27 November 2025
Venue Dubai World Trade Centre, Dubai, UAE
Category Building and Construction

The Big 5 Global is a unique trade event dedicated to construction and urban development. It is a remarkable platform to discover the latest solutions and insights regarding building operations, from planning to construction, and everything in between. You can meet with global suppliers and strengthen your network in the urban development community. Participate in Big5 Global, showcase your products and solutions to potential customers, and grab new opportunities.

3. Automechanika Dubai 2025

Dates 09 - 11 December 2025
Venue Dubai World Trade Centre, Dubai, UAE
Category Auto and Automotive

Automechanika Dubai is a globally recognized trade event within the automotive aftermarket industry. It features live demonstrations of a wide range of products and solutions for automotive aftermarket professionals. You can showcase innovations, build strategic partnerships, and drive business. It offers a great environment for networking across the industry. Leverage the benefits Automechanika Dubai offers and get ahead of the competition in your industry.

4. AEEDC Dubai 2026

Dates 19 - 21 January 2026
Venue Dubai World Trade Centre, Dubai, UAE
Category Dental Industry

AEEDC is a global platform for dentistry professionals and practitioners. Known as “The largest annual scientific dental conference and exhibition in the world”, it attracts a wide range of dentistry experts, academicians, and industry professionals from all across the globe. They come together to share solutions and the latest trends in the industry. Be a part of AEEDC Dubai to gain insights into the world of oral and maxillofacial surgery.

5. Gulfood 2026

Dates 26 - 30 January 2026
Venue Dubai World Trade Centre + Dubai Expo City, Dubai, UAE
Category Food & Beverages

Gulfood is one of the world’s largest annual food and hospitality shows. This international fair for food and beverages features food, drink, ingredients, equipment, services, skills, and education. You can attend demonstrations and world-class culinary competitions covering platters of all manner, ice carvings, buffet platters, pastry, and chocolate making. The exhibitors and visitors include chefs, pastry chefs, bakers, cooks, students, and professionals from across the globe.

6. World Health Expo (formerly Arab Health) 2026

Dates 09 - 12 February 2026
Venue Dubai Exhibition Centre, Dubai, UAE
Category Medical and Pharma

The World Health Expo Dubai (WHX Dubai) is a highly anticipated event for healthcare professionals worldwide. You can explore the trends and revolutionary tech to optimize your healthcare practices. Gain valuable industry insights from eminent experts and build connections with international industry leaders. Leverage the exceptional learning, trading, and networking opportunities and acquire an outstanding reputation in the global healthcare community by participating in the WHX Dubai.

7. Seatrade Maritime Logistics Middle East 2027

Dates 11 - 13 May 2027
Venue Dubai World Trade Centre, Dubai, UAE
Category Maritime Industry

The Seatrade Maritime Logistics Middle East is the largest maritime trade event that focuses on progress in the maritime industry. It attracts ship owners, operators, and managers, along with industry professionals. Businesses and professionals from local and international maritime markets gather here to do business across the Middle East market and network with key international stakeholders.

8. Cabsat 2026

Dates 20 - 22 May 2026
Venue Dubai World Trade Centre, Dubai, UAE
Category Digital Media

The region’s largest event for creative, broadcast, and satellite communities is the leading show for innovation and engaging experiences in digital media. More than 1100 global exhibitors and 18000 visitors from 120+ countries come together to discuss the most innovative solutions with leaders and professionals across the industry. Connect with content creators, distributors, and key decision-makers to gain insights on best practices and trends in the media industry.

9. Index 2026

Dates 04 - 06 June 2026
Venue Dubai World Trade Centre, Dubai, UAE
Category Furniture and Interior Design

Index showcases the entire spectrum of furniture and interior design for the hospitality industry. You can find accessories and equipment from categories like kitchen & bathroom, office, retail furniture, homeware, lighting, furnishing textiles, and more. Furniture manufacturers & suppliers come to showcase their latest collection to commercial and retail buyers. You can gain valuable information on the latest trends in the furniture and interior decoration market.

10. World Vape Show 2026

Dates 10 - 12 June 2026
Venue Dubai World Trade Centre, Dubai, UAE
Category e-cigarette

The World Vape Show is dedicated to e-cigarettes, vaping devices, and e-liquids to curb smoking and stop the online sale of unregulated devices. It is the first vape expo in Dubai, bringing together manufacturers and suppliers. This event provides them with an opportunity to showcase their products to an audience including retailers, wholesalers, distributors, and vaping enthusiasts.
