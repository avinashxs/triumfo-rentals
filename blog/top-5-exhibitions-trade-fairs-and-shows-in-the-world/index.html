Top 5 Upcoming Trade Shows in The World

Trade shows offer a multitude of opportunities to exhibiting companies. They are touchpoints between businesses and buyers. These events help increase your brand awareness and offer networking opportunities. Exhibitors wait for such opportunities every year. Many such outstanding events keep getting organised in countries such as the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, India, Spain, and so on.

Whether you are an exporter, importer, or any businessperson, you should participate in international trade shows to showcase your products and achieve your short-term and long-term business goals. We have enlisted the world’s best trade shows belonging to diverse industries. Find the best one for your business, get a well-crafted trade show exhibit booth, and leverage the benefits of these events for boosting your business growth.

Here are the five most popular business events in the world scheduled in the upcoming days.

1. IFE: International Food & Drink Event 2026

Date: 30 March - 01 April 2026
Location: Excel London, London, UK
Focus: Food and beverage items

The International Food & Drink Event (IFE) is the largest and fundamental food and drink event. It is a brilliant trade platform where food and drink suppliers from around the world come together. You can meet buyers and chefs from different departments such as retail, food service, manufacturing, distribution, and wholesale.

More than 25,000 visitors from 100+ countries attend this sourcing platform for food, drink & hospitality products, divided into 15 distinct product categories. As an exhibitor at this show, you get a brilliant opportunity to connect with buyers across retail, wholesale, import, export, and hospitality sectors. You must exhibit at the IFE to acquire new business opportunities.

2. Global Seafood Marketplace 2026

Date: 21 - 23 April 2026
Location: Fira Barcelona Gran Via Venue, Barcelona, Spain
Focus: Seafood products and processing

Global Seafood Marketplace is the world’s largest seafood trade fair, where more than 2000 companies from over 89 countries participate. It attracts 35,400+ seafood professionals and buyers from all levels of the supply chain. You can explore the latest food trends as a visitor here. You can showcase your innovations to a highly interested audience at this event.

This event offers unparalleled sourcing opportunities to seafood buyers. You can’t miss exhibiting at this trade fair if you are in the seafood business and want to showcase your seafood products, equipment, and services to an interested audience.

3. ITB Berlin 2026

Date: 03 – 05 March 2026
Location: Berlin ExpoCenter City, Berlin, Germany
Focus: Tour destinations and travel solutions

The Internationale Tourismus-Börse Berlin (ITB Berlin) is a prominent travel trade show. It showcases holiday destinations, adventure tourism, business travel, luxury travel, medical tourism, and travel technology. At the ITB Berlin Convention, experts discuss the trends and innovations in the travel sector.

As an exhibition, it hosts 5,800+ exhibitors from 170+ countries. It welcomes 100,000 visitors from 190+ countries. As a convention, it features 200 educational sessions led by 400 leading industry speakers and attended by 24000 participants. Participate in this prestigious travel show to establish your business among the pioneers of the global travel industry.

4. International Production & Processing Expo (IPPE) 2026

Date: 27 - 29 January 2026
Location: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, USA
Focus: Animal food products and processes

International Production and Processing Expo (IPPE) is the most anticipated trade event in the animal food industry. It is dedicated to poultry, egg, meat, and animal food-based businesses. This expo is an outstanding platform to find highly potent solutions for optimizing business practices, updating operations, and staying competitive in the animal food and protein industries.

You get the opportunity to showcase the latest products, services, technologies, research, and processes to interested meat, poultry and egg, and animal food professionals. You can connect with your peers, find new suppliers and vendors, and expand your business network through this event

5. Plastivision India: International Plastics Exhibition & Conference 2027

Date: 21 - 25 January 2027
Location: Bombay Exhibition Centre (BEC), Mumbai, India
Focus: Plastic products, services, and processes

Plastivision India (PVI) is a major plastics show in the world. It features exhibitors across a diverse range of plastics solution categories, such as raw material, flexible packaging, additives and compounds, manufacturing machinery, post-processing machinery, automation, finished goods, recycling, etc. It features an international B2B meet, a CEO roundtable, a conference, and a consultant clinic.

PVI Event features 1200+ exhibitors from 20 countries and welcomes 2,30,000+ visitors from 80+ countries. Participate in this global plastics trade show to gain tremendous brand visibility, strengthen your relationships in the industry, and establish your brand at the forefront of the industry.

Expert Tips for Success at Global Trade Shows

In-depth Research in Advance: You should understand the event theme, audience, and competitors before starting preparations.

Set Clear Goals: You must define measurable trade show objectives (leads, sales, partnerships) and make strategies while keeping them in consideration.

Get Engaging Booth Design: You should include attractive displays and interactive elements in your custom trade show display.

Strong Branding: You need to ensure consistent branding and messaging across all trade show materials.

Product Demos: You must showcase the benefits of your products with live demonstrations in your 20x20 booth.
