Top 5 Fashion & Beauty Trade Shows in the USA 2025-2026

When you are in a dynamic industry like beauty and fashion, you must seek effective ways to get ahead of your competitors. The best way is to represent your business at a beauty and fashion trade show. Such events offer exposure to potential customers and opportunities to meet with potential business partners.

If your business belongs to this industry and is based in the USA, you can find many beauty trade fairs to participate in. Here are the five most sought-after trade shows in this industry scheduled in the upcoming months of 2025-2026. Get smartly crafted trade show booth rentals in the USA to showcase your brand at these excellent business platforms.

1. The Makeup Show Houston 2025

Scheduled Date 20 - 21 September 2025
Location Hilton Houston Post Oak by the Galleria, Houston, TX, USA
Focus Makeup artists, beauty experts, skincare & haircare products

The Makeup Show Houston is one of the distinguished makeup trade shows that connects top professionals, renowned brands, and passionate artists from the beauty industry for an immersive experience. It features the latest trends and cutting-edge products across the industry. This allows you to gain expert insights through masterclasses, live demos, and panel discussions led by top makeup artists and industry leaders.

With a vibrant atmosphere filled with creativity and innovation, this event offers unparalleled networking opportunities and exclusive access to pro-only discounts. Whether you are a seasoned professional or an aspiring artist, The Makeup Show Houston is the ultimate destination to connect, learn, and elevate your artistry to the next level.

2. LA TEXTILE Show 2025

Scheduled Date 07 - 09 October 2025
Location California Market Center (CMC), Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus Fashion design, fabrics, and textiles

LA Textile is the premier West Coast trade show for textile, design, and fashion sourcing. It brings together industry professionals from around the world. Showcasing the latest in fabric innovations, trims, and sustainable materials, the event serves as a dynamic hub for designers, manufacturers, and brands seeking inspiration and high-quality resources. With a focus on creativity and sustainability, it features a range of textiles from global suppliers.

Beyond sourcing, LA Textile features insightful seminars, trend forecasts, and networking opportunities that connect emerging designers with industry leaders. As one of the most anticipated Los Angeles fashion trade shows, this event fosters collaboration, innovation, and business growth in the ever-evolving fashion and textile sector.

3. The International Gem & Jewelry Show (Fall) 2025

Scheduled Date 12 - 14 December 2025
Location Donald E. Stephens Convention Center, Rosemont, IL, USA
Focus Consumer jewelry, gemstones, and handcrafted art

International Gem & Jewelry Show (Fall) 2025 Rosemont showcases an impressive range of gemstones, fine jewelry, and unique artisan pieces. This event brings together top designers, wholesalers, and collectors, offering an unparalleled selection of high-quality gems, beads, and exquisite jewelry. Whether you are a seasoned buyer or an individual simply looking for a special piece, you will find a stunning array of precious ornaments at every price point.

InterGem Jewelry Show offers a one-of-a-kind shopping experience where you can meet expert jewelers, learn about the latest trends, and even find rare, handcrafted designs. Don't miss this opportunity to explore, shop, and experience the brilliance of fine craftsmanship!

4. International Beauty Show (IBS) New York 2026

Scheduled Date 08 - 10 March 2026
Location Javits Convention Center, New York, NY, USA
Focus Education, training, and products for makeup and beauty treatments

The International Beauty Show (IBS) New York is an exclusive event where beauty professionals, brands, and industry experts come together to explore the latest trends, innovations, and techniques. It is counted among the top beauty trade shows where top-tier exhibitors showcase cutting-edge products related to hair, nails, skincare, and cosmetics. You can explore exclusive deals, network with leading professionals, and gain hands-on experience with the newest tools and technologies.

Beyond the show floor, IBS New York offers world-class education through live demonstrations, expert-led workshops, and business-building seminars. These attributes have positioned it among the most sought-after beauty product trade shows. If you are a stylist, salon owner, or beauty entrepreneur, this event is the perfect opportunity to elevate your craft.

5. America's Beauty Show 2026

Scheduled Date 18- 20 April 2026
Location Donald E. Stephens Convention Center, Rosemont, IL, USA
Focus Workshops and training sessions for cosmetologists and beauty professionals

America's Beauty Show is the ultimate destination for beauty professionals looking to elevate their craft. This industry event gathers top brands, educators, and artists to showcase the latest trends, techniques, and innovations in hair, makeup, skincare, and nails. From hands-on workshops to live demonstrations, attendees gain valuable insights and skills to stay ahead in the ever-evolving beauty world.

Whether you are a stylist, salon owner, or beauty enthusiast, this event offers endless networking opportunities, exclusive product launches, and expert-led sessions. Leverage one of the most-renowned cosmetics trade shows in the USA for professional benefits. Experience the energy, creativity, and inspiration that make America’s Beauty Show a must-attend for industry professionals!

Tips for Success at Beauty Product Trade Shows

  • Showcase Your Prime Beauty Products: Highlight your bestsellers or latest launches prominently to capture interest quickly.
  • Conduct Live Demos: Set up mini-stations for makeup application, skincare trials, or quick tutorials to show product effectiveness.
  • Create a Visually Stunning Booth: Use sleek design, brand-consistent colors, and strong lighting to attract attendees from a distance.
  • Engage Influencers & Artists: Invite beauty influencers or local MUAs to demonstrate your products. This approach boosts credibility and reach.
  • Use Samples & Giveaways: Offer small product samples or gifts with QR codes linking to your official/e-commerce website or social media.
