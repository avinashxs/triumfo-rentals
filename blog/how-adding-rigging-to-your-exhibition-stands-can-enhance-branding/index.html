How Rigging Your Exhibition Stands Enhances Branding?

Want to know about a booth design element that has the power to enhance the effectiveness of your entire trade show campaign? Rigging is that design element and the answer you are looking for! It can enhance the overall look of your exhibition stand while drawing attention from visitors across the exhibition hall.

What Does Rigging in Exhibition Stands Mean?

Rigging in an exhibition stand means suspending booth elements or hanging objects from the ceiling. Hanging booth elements like signage, banners, and lighting make your booth visually appealing and impactful while using overhead space efficiently. However, there are a few cons also related to rigging, along with so many pros.

In this blog, we will discuss the benefits of rigging along with a few of its drawbacks.

Benefits of Rigging Your Exhibition Stands

Rigging has so many advantages that it would be worth every cent spent. Let’s discuss the benefits of rigging your exhibition stand.

  • The height of your exhibition stand can be extended up to 3-4m, which would be helpful if your exhibition stand is comparatively smaller than other booths.
  • If you are seeking an effective method to gain visual prominence from all angles to maximize visitor traffic at your exhibition stand, rigging is the best option.
  • If you want to attract visitors from the other side of the exhibition hall or other aisles as they walk around, rigging can help you in the best possible way.
  • Rigging comes in all sizes and shapes. These suspended structures can be circular, angular, or some other shape. They also come in a range of finishes and materials.
  • You can also add lighting equipment to your rigged structure. It will enhance banners, branding, and the illumination in your stand.
  • Rigging enables you to take your exhibition booth ideas to the next level and appear distinct from other exhibitors.
  • Rigging allows you to take complete advantage of your entire available space and use it to its highest potential.
  • It adds elegance to your exhibition stand and makes your brand stand out from your competitors.

Drawbacks of Rigging Your Exhibition Stand

There are only a few issues with rigging an exhibition stand. You should know them to get the best aspects of that booth design element. They are mentioned below:

  • Rigging can impact the total cost of your exhibition stand. So, if you have a small budget, you can ignore this exhibition stand element.
  • Exhibitors often ignore other important things that are much more important than rigging.
  • There are certain venues with height restrictions, and organizers do not allow rigging above the booth.
