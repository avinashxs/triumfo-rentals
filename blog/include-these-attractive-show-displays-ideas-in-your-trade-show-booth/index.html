5 Fantastic Display Ideas to Make Your Trade Show Booth More Attractive

A trade show display is a necessary part of the trade show because it is a major attention puller at such events. There is a wide range of trade show displays available in the market and as an exhibitor, you get several choices to choose from.

While some go for customized choices with a custom trade show booth, others would opt for a standard display. However, the main objective of all the exhibitors participating in the trade show is to have a display that immediately grabs the attention of the visitors and enhances the traffic at their trade show booths.

To help you choose the most effective and suitable trade show display, we are mentioning the list of best display ideas. You can rely on these suggestions to make your booth look more attractive.

1. Hanging Displays

As their name suggests, hanging displays could be signage, booth banners, AVs, and much more. These are suspended from the ceiling of the trade show area with the help of cords and wires. Also, these are available in a range of sizes and shapes including rings, pyramids, squares, and customized shapes. These are highly favorable for trade show exhibits as they are visually attractive, lightweight, and easily collapsible.

2. Tension Fabric Displays

The tension fabric display is made up of a metal framework that stretches the fabric and creates a wall-like seamless structure. Furthermore, you can also attach lighting fixtures around and above the framework to illuminate these displays. Also, these displays can be used to install TV screens and shelves. The best part about them is that they are easily collapsible, travel-friendly, and can be customized as per your brand’s or trade show requirements.

3. Modular Displays

Modular displays are one of the most common trade show displays. These are highly favored choices among most trade show booth designs due to their structure. A modular display is a set of frames and panels that collectively generate a structural framework. These displays are easy to assemble and dismantle, which makes them easy to travel with. Also, they come in a wide range of sizes and shapes and are lightweight.

4. Pipe and Drape

The frame of pipe and drapes is built by joining hollow metal tubes at the corner and upright bases. After this, drapes are put over this structure which hides the pipe framework and separates booths from one another. Most of the time, exhibitors employ this type of display to have their trade show pop up inside the frame. Whereas, some exhibitors employ these to connect their trade show graphics such as trade show booth banners and other signage used by them.

5. Banner Stands

Banner stands are the most commonly used trade show displays to grab the attention of visitors. These types of displays are frameworks built of plastic or metal. You just need to attach the fabric to the framework and your banner stand is ready.
