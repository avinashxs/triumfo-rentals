Top 5 Animals & Pets Trade Shows in the USA in 2025

The United States is home to some of the world's leading trade shows dedicated to animals and pets. At these events, innovation meets opportunity. They offer a remarkable platform to manufacturers, retailers, veterinarians, industry professionals, and pet enthusiasts to connect, showcase, and succeed. The animal and pet industry is thriving, and these events are perfect for discovering the latest products, networking with key players, and growing your business.

Participate in the right trade fairs to boost your business growth. However, creating a solid first impression is essential for exhibitors, and an engaging booth design can be the key to standing out. You must invest in a stunning exhibit rental in the USA for success at these shows.

Let's explore the USA's top 5 animal and pet trade shows, you can consider participating in, along with an expert tip to deliver a tremendous performance in the respective trade fair.

1. SuperZoo Expo 2025

Dates: 13 – 15 August 2025
Location: Mandalay Bay Convention Center, Las Vegas, NV, USA
Focus: Pet retail

SuperZoo is a leading trade show for pet retailers across the world. It focuses on the latest products, grooming trends, and animal wellness. It’s the go-to event for pet retailers, providing opportunities to connect with manufacturers and stay updated on consumer trends. It features 65+ education sessions led by industry experts. Participate in this expo to acquire actionable knowledge and apply it to optimize your business operations.

Exhibitor Tip

Use a bold trade show booth design with clear branding to attract busy attendees.

2. Poultry Tech Summit (PTS) 2025

Dates: 03 - 05 November 2025
Location: Atlanta Airport Marriott, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Poultry supply chain

Poultry Tech Summit (PTS) is a distinguished event for poultry supply chain professionals. It is a platform to address their challenges and solutions. This summit attracts professionals, entrepreneurs, inventors, researchers, and technology providers across the poultry industry. This event serves as a brilliant platform to showcase innovative technologies and their commercial applications in poultry-based operations. Participate in PTS to leverage the networking and learning opportunities to boost poultry production.

Exhibitor Tip

Get an engaging trade show exhibition rental in the USA to create a family-friendly, interactive booth experience.

3. Veterinary Meeting & Expo (VMX) 2026

Dates: 17 - 21 January 2026
Location: Orange County Convention Center in Orlando, FL, USA
Focus: Veterinary medicine and equipment

Veterinary Meeting & Expo is one of the USA's largest veterinary conferences and trade shows. It caters to veterinarians and animal health professionals. This event offers the learning, tools, and experiences to improve your veterinary practices. You can meet with the leading players in your profession. VMX provides access to cutting-edge veterinary products and education, making it an essential event for animal health professionals.

Exhibitor Tip

A custom trade show booth with professional and educational displays can help highlight your expertise in the veterinary field.

4. GROOM’D 2026

Dates: 13 - 15 March 2026
Location: Georgia International Convention Center, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus: Pet grooming professionals

Groom’d is a premier pet trade show dedicated to pet grooming. It showcases the latest equipment and education to improve grooming skills and boost your business. 4,000+ professionals visit this trade fair to explore the grooming supplies, accessories, and technology showcased by 115+ exhibitors. If you are a professional groomer, kennel operator, or pet care professional, attend this expo to discover new trends and tools and beat your competition.

Exhibitor Tip:

Opt for a cost-effective trade show exhibition rental in the USA for high ROI performance.

5. Global Pet Expo 2026

Dates: 25 – 27 March 2026
Location: Orange County Convention Center, Orlando, FL, USA
Focus: Pet products, services, and trends

Global Pet Expo is one of the most anticipated pet industry trade shows. It showcases various pet products, from food and toys to health innovations. With over 1,100 exhibitors and 17,000+ attendees from across the globe, this show is a hub for innovation and industry networking. This expo witnesses 1,000+ new product launches and features 50+ educational sessions by eminent experts.

Exhibitor Tip

Invest in a custom trade show booth with interactive features to highlight your product's unique benefits.
