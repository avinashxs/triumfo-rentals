Top 5 Food & Beverages Trade Shows in the USA

The food and beverage industry is a vibrant sector that comprises numerous opportunities and embraces innovation. As a decision-maker or owner of a business belonging to this sector, you have to be aware of the latest developments in the industry to remain competent. The F&B trade shows and exhibitions offer not only an extraordinary platform to showcase what your business has to offer to your potential customers, but also provide an excellent opportunity to stay relevant with the times.

Here are the 5 highly anticipated food and beverage trade shows in the USA scheduled in the upcoming days. If you are looking to exhibit and expand in the USA, these events provide the perfect platform to fulfill these objectives.

1. World Dairy Expo 2025

Scheduled Date 30 September - 3 October 2025
Location Alliant Energy Center, Madison, Wisconsin, USA
Focus Dairy products and cattle

World Dairy Expo is the premier event for dairy experts. It attracts dairy industry leaders, producers, and innovators from around the world. This global gathering showcases the latest advancements in dairy technology, genetics, and herd management, offering a unique opportunity to explore cutting-edge equipment, products, and research. With world-class dairy cattle competitions, educational seminars, and networking events, the expo is a hub for knowledge-sharing and business growth.

You can engage with top exhibitors, gain insights from industry experts, and witness the future of dairy unfold. Whether you are a producer, researcher, or supplier, this event provides invaluable connections and resources.

2. International Production & Processing Expo (IPPE) 2026

Scheduled Date 27 - 29 January 2026
Location Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, GA, USA
Focus Animal food, protein solutions, and innovations

The International Production & Processing Expo (IPPE) is the world’s largest annual event for enthusiasts in the animal food, meat, and poultry industries. It brings together the key decision-makers, suppliers, and innovators in the industry and provides them with a dynamic platform for networking, business growth, and knowledge exchange. With a strong focus on industry advancements, it showcases cutting-edge technology, equipment, and solutions that drive efficiency and sustainability.

You can explore a vast exhibition floor, participate in educational programs, and engage in discussions on emerging trends and regulatory updates at IPPE 2026. Participate and leverage this global hub for fostering partnerships and discovering the latest innovations shaping the future of food production.

4. New York Restaurant Show 2026

Scheduled Date 08 - 10 March 2026
Location Jacob Javits Convention Center, New York, NY, USA
Focus Restaurant and food service

The New York Restaurant Show is a leading event for restaurant owners, chefs, and food service experts looking to stay ahead of the competition in the industry. Featuring cutting-edge products, innovative technology, and expert-led educational sessions, this event is the ultimate destination to discover the latest trends and solutions in hospitality. From specialty food and beverage to kitchen equipment and front-of-house innovations, you can explore a diverse range of products and network with industry leaders.

With live cooking demonstrations, panel discussions, and business growth strategies, the show provides valuable insights and opportunities to elevate any restaurant or food business. Whether you are seeking inspiration or practical solutions, this event is a must-attend for those passionate about culinary excellence.

3. Seafood Expo North America (SENA) 2026

Scheduled Date 15 - 17 March 2026
Location Boston Convention and Exhibition Center, Boston, MA, USA
Focus Fish, seafood, and seafood-related products and services

Seafood Expo North America is the exclusive seafood trade event, connecting suppliers, buyers, and industry specialists from across the globe. It showcases a vast selection of fresh, frozen, packaged, and value-added seafood products, along with cutting-edge processing and packaging solutions. The event offers an unparalleled opportunity to network, discover new business opportunities, and stay ahead of market trends.

Along with the exhibition floor, the expo features an extensive conference program covering key industry topics such as sustainability, regulations, and emerging consumer trends. Such attributes make the SENA event the ultimate destination for seafood masters looking to connect, innovate, and grow in a dynamic marketplace.

5. Sweets & Snacks Expo 2026

Scheduled Date 19 - 21 May 2026
Location Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC), Las Vegas, NV, USA
Focus Confectionery and snacks

The Sweets & Snacks Expo is the ultimate gathering for confectionery and snack industry professionals. It brings together top brands, emerging innovators, and retail buyers from around the world under one roof. This premier event showcases the latest trends, flavors, and products shaping the future of sweets and snacks, offering unparalleled networking and business opportunities.

You can explore 1000+ exhibits of innovative confectionery and savory snacks, discover groundbreaking product launches, and gain insights from industry experts through educational sessions. Whether you are a retailer, distributor, or manufacturer, this expo is the perfect place to connect, learn, and get ahead in the ever-evolving confectionery market.

Effective Tips to Ensure a Successful Campaign in F&B Business Events

  • Leverage Social Media & PR: Announce your participation across platforms. Share behind-the-scenes content, live updates, and invite followers to visit your booth.
  • Design a Bespoke Booth: Get a booth design that reflects your brand’s culinary identity. Include visual storytelling, enticing food displays, and interactive tasting zones.
  • Train Your Staff Thoroughly: Equip your booth staff members with brand knowledge, talking points, and hospitality etiquette to engage and convert visitors effectively.
  • Offer Live Demonstrations or Tastings: Food and beverage expos thrive on sensory engagement. Let your booth visitors sample your menu or watch a chef demo for a memorable experience.
  • Network Strategically: Schedule meetings with buyers, food critics, distributors, and potential franchisees. Use the exhibitor list and event app to plan in advance.
