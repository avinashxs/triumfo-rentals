Top 10 Trade Shows in Los Angeles 2025-2026

Los Angeles is a global financial centre and the largest manufacturing center in the US. It would not be surprising if we say that this Californian city offers incredible opportunities to businesses across various sectors. Whether you belong to the retail, healthcare, automobile, or renewable energy industry, you can conveniently expand your market reach through the city. All you have to do is find the right trade event for your business, get a visually striking trade show booth in Los Angeles to represent your brand, and you are ready to beat the competition. Here are the ten most-awaited trade fairs in Los Angeles scheduled in the upcoming days.

1. Greenbuild International Conference & Expo 2025

Date: 04 - 07 November 2025
Location: Los Angeles Convention Center, Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Green building professionals

Greenbuild International Conference & Expo is a highly anticipated event for green building professionals. Experts across the industry participate in this event to share valuable insights on designing, constructing, and operating real estate assets while maximizing environmental sustainability and energy efficiency. You can meet eminent industry partners and find the most effective solutions to achieve your desired sustainability certification or goal for your building.

This expo attracts 10,000+ green building professionals from different parts of the world. More than 250+ exhibitors from 59+ countries showcase their cutting-edge green building solutions here. Its extensive conference program features 100+ educational sessions and 300+ industry speakers. You must attend this event to get to the forefront of the industry.

2. Renegade Craft Event Los Angeles (Fall + Winter) 2025

Date: 08 - 09 November 2025
Location: Santa Monica Airport, Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Arts, artists, and creative community

Renegade Event LA is a unique event dedicated to contemporary craft and design. You can meet 275+ curated artists at this vibrant art fair. It serves as an exceptional platform and a thriving marketplace for the creative community to showcase their art. It connects the local market and attracts tens of thousands of attendees, including potential customers in a discovery mindset.

By exhibiting at this craft event, you showcase your artefacts and creative items to a trendsetting audience and uplift your business. If you want to build and strengthen meaningful relationships across the creative community, this is a perfect event for you to participate in.

3. Los Angeles Auto Show 2025

Date: 21 - 30 November 2025
Location: LA Convention Center, Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Automotive and mobility innovations

The Los Angeles Auto Show is a prominent B2B and B2C platform for the automobile industry. It showcases the cutting-edge automotive and mobility innovations. Being one of the most attended events, it brings together the entire ecosystem. Along with tens of thousands of auto industry professionals, decision-makers, and influencers, this event also features around 5000 media personnel from 50+ countries.

If you want to experience the convergence of technology and the automobile, the LA Auto Show is a must-attend event for you. As an exhibitor, you get to showcase your automotive products and innovations in front of hundreds of thousands of qualified car shoppers, auto fans, and enthusiasts. Participate in this trade show to grow industry networking, gain global media exposure, and significantly elevate your brand value.

4. FitExpo Los Angeles 2026

Date: 10 - 11 January 2026
Location: LA Convention Center, Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Health and fitness knowledge and products

The Fit Expo is considered the largest fitness event across the West Coast of the USA. It brings together thousands of fitness enthusiasts under one roof. Whether you are a bodybuilder, powerlifter, an athlete, a jiu-jitsu practitioner, a personal trainer, an exercise instructor, or deals in functional fitness, this expo has something to offer everyone. You can meet and converse with fitness influencers and attend seminars to gain valuable health and wellness insights.

At the FitExpo, you can explore new health, wellness, and fitness products and services for yourself as well as your pets. It also features free workout classes, cooking demos, and live sporting competitions. If you deal in health supplements, energy drinks, gym clothes, protein bars, electrolytes, and other fitness solutions, this event is worth participating in.

5. ViVE Event 2026

Date: 22 - 25 February 2026
Location: Los Angeles Convention Center, Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Healthcare business

ViVE Event is a distinguished trade show for digital health professionals. Here, the term ViVE is an acronym for Virtual Integrated Value-based Experience. This event showcases value-based healthcare practices and related strategies. It attracts professionals and 500+ businesses across the healthcare industry, including solution providers, health startups, investors, policymakers, C-suite executives, senior digital health leaders, and buyers.

Some of the major segments of ViVe Show are the AI Zone, CyberSecurity Zone, and InteropNow! Zone, HealthTech Showcase, Impact Program, Nusres @ ViVe, Startup Pitch Competitions, and Best in Klas Awards. If you are a decision-maker from a healthcare business and want to build meaningful relationships across the community, this is a must-attend event for you.

6. Los Angeles Travel & Adventure Show 2026

Date: 07 - 08 March 2026
Location: Long Beach Convention Center, Long Beach, CA, USA
Focus: Tour, travel, and holiday destinations and services

The Los Angeles Travel and Adventure Show is a distinguished trade fair for travel and tour-based businesses. It features numerous international and domestic holiday destinations. This event attracts 450+ tour operators, travel service providers, and cruise lines from around the world. You can explore countless options for your next holiday and learn about the latest trends in the industry by attending 60+ travel seminars.

The LA T&A Show also features many fun activities, including dance performances, contests with travel-oriented prizes and deals, and many more interactive activities. You can meet with travel experts and celebrities, including Rick Steves, Josh Gates, and Pauline Frommer. If you are a tour and travel solution provider seeking opportunities to connect with potential travelers actively planning their trips, you must participate in this show.

7. LA Market Week 2026

Date: 09 - 11 March 2026
Location: California Market Center (CMC), Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Women’s, Men’s, and Kids’ apparel and fashion accessories

The LA Market Week is one of the most renowned events in the fashion industry. It showcases the latest apparel, textiles, and design resources for men, women, and kids. Some notable brands participating in this event include Levi’s, Free People, Mavi, Jack & Jones, Paige, Vero Moda, Bailey Rose, Frank Lyman, Molo, Gigi Moda, Kenzo Kids, and others. They showcase their fresh selection of apparel and fashion accessories for diverse boutique and consumer tastes.

Whether you are an up-and-coming designer or a well-established fashion brand, this event offers a perfect platform to showcase your creativity with craftsmanship to a highly relevant audience. Participate in this event to find new buyers, build real connections across the industry, and take your business to new heights.

8. OFC Conference 2026

Date: 15 - 19 March 2026
Location: Los Angeles Convention Center, Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Optical networking and communications

The Optical Fiber Communication (OFC) Conference and Exposition is a prestigious event belonging to the optical networking and communications industry. This event serves as an excellent platform to showcase fiber products, optical systems, devices, components, related test equipment, and software. It attracts the finest experts in the industry to share the latest technologies, developments, and invaluable insights into the field.

Around 690 leading companies across the ecosystem showcase their optical networking and communications solutions to 16000+ visitors from 90+ countries. This technical conference program includes interactive workshops, symposia, tutorials, and special sessions on dynamic topics, from generative AI and ML for optical networking to subsea networks, space optics, and so on. Participate in this event to reshape your future in the industry.

9. The NAMA Show 2026

Date: 22 - 24 April 2026
Location: Los Angeles Convention Center, Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Convenience services

The NAMA Show is an outstanding trade event for professionals and operators in the convenience services industry. It is dedicated to unattended retail, micro markets, pantries, vending, and kiosk-based services. Organized by the National Automatic Merchandising Association (NAMA), this show features the latest technology and innovative products. It brings remarkable learning and networking opportunities to the participants.

The NAMA Show features 400+ exhibiting companies, serves as a launchpad for 100+ products, and features 20+ educational sessions. You can meet and network with 5000+ top professionals and decision-makers across the industry. If you want to boost your business growth and deliver distinguished consumer experiences, you must attend this show.

10. ATD International Conference & Expo 2026

Date: 17 - 20 May 2026
Location: Los Angeles Convention Center, Los Angeles, CA, USA
Focus: Talent development in workplaces

The Association for Talent Development (ATD) International Conference and Expo is a highly anticipated event for professionals across the talent development industry. It attracts talent development managers, frontline managers, trainers, performance consultants, instructional designers, workplace learning professionals, and so on. It offers learning opportunities through a variety of courses and certifications.

This conference features educational sessions across diverse and dynamic topics, such as AI resources, coaching, higher education, diversity, equity, and inclusion, talent development leaders, talent strategy and management, training and facilitation, and so on. If you are a professional or an organization working to improve knowledge, skills, and abilities in the workplace, you can’t miss that event.

Crucial Tips for Success at Trade Shows and Exhibitions in Los Angeles

Know Your Trade Show Audience: Research attendee demographics and tailor the messaging and design of your trade show exhibit booth accordingly.

Book Your Space and Services Early: Secure your booth space, lodging, and shipping in advance to avoid last-minute hassle due to high demand.

Design Your Booth to Stand Out: Use creative visuals, lighting, and interactive elements strategically in your 10x10 booth and bigger ones to attract foot traffic.

Engage with Purpose: Train your booth staff to communicate your messaging and value clearly and professionally to your booth visitors.

Leverage Social Media: Promote your booth, giveaways, or demos on social media and relevant digital media channels to boost footfall.

Network Strategically: Attend networking events and mixers at LA trade fairs to build meaningful connections across the industry.

Follow Up Promptly: Nurture leads quickly post-show for maximum conversion and ensure generating an encouraging ROI from the event.
