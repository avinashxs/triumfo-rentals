Top 5 Auto & Automotive Trade Shows in the USA

The auto and automotive industry is a dynamic segment. It is highly competitive and constantly witnessing novel and innovative advancements. If you are an entrepreneur or business owner in this industry and want to stay ahead of the competition, participating in trade shows in the USA can be a beneficial step. It provides opportunities to connect with an untapped segment of potential customers and expand your professional network within and beyond your industry.

If you are now exploring prominent trade shows in this industry, you don’t have to look elsewhere. Here are the 5 most significant auto and automotive trade shows scheduled during the next few months across the USA.

1. Auto.AI USA

Scheduled Date 30 June - 01 July 2025
Location Hyatt Regency Downtown Soma, San Francisco, CA, USA
Focus AI, Machine Learning, Neural Networks & Cognitive Computing

Auto.AI USA is the premier event for innovators, engineers, and industry leaders shaping the future of autonomous driving. This conference dives deep into AI-driven vehicle automation, advanced perception systems, and the latest breakthroughs in machine learning for self-driving technology. You can explore cutting-edge research, real-world applications, and challenges in bringing fully autonomous vehicles to market.

Connecting experts from automotive OEMs, tech companies, and research institutions, Auto.AI USA fosters collaboration and knowledge-sharing. The event features insightful keynotes, interactive panels, and networking opportunities, making it the go-to platform for those driving the next generation of AI-powered mobility.

2. Automotive Aftermarket Products Expo (AAPEX) 2025

Scheduled Date 04 - 06 November 2025
Location Venetian Expo and Caesar's Forum, Las Vegas, NV, USA
Focus Automotive aftermarket products and solutions

The Automotive Aftermarket Products Expo (AAPEX) is the exclusive global event showcasing the latest innovations, technologies, and solutions in the automotive aftermarket industry. This expo attracts top manufacturers, suppliers, and industry professionals. It offers a dynamic platform for networking, business growth, and discovering cutting-edge products that drive the future of vehicle maintenance and repair.

Attendees can explore a vast array of exhibits featuring advanced automotive parts, diagnostic tools, and service solutions while gaining valuable insights from industry leaders through expert-led sessions and hands-on demonstrations. As one of the leading automotive aftermarket trade shows, AAPEX is the ultimate destination for those looking to stay ahead in the evolving automotive aftermarket sector.

3. Specialty Equipment Market Association (SEMA) 2025

Scheduled Date 04 - 07 November 2025
Location Las Vegas Convention Center(LVCC), Las Vegas, NV, USA

The Specialty Equipment Market Association (SEMA) Show is one of the most anticipated automotive industry trade shows in the USA. It is the ultimate gathering for automotive professionals, industry leaders, manufacturers, and enthusiasts. It showcases the latest innovations in aftermarket parts, performance upgrades, and cutting-edge automotive technology. Attendees gain exclusive access to groundbreaking products, expert insights, and networking opportunities that shape the future of the automotive industry.

Being one of the distinguished B2B automotive industry events, the SEMA show brings together tens of thousands of buyers and decision-makers, making it a valuable platform for exhibitors.

4. Discover Boating Miami International Boat Show 2026

Scheduled Date 11 - 15 February 2026
Location Six locations across Miami, FL, USA
Focus Boating and marine products

The Discover Boating Miami International Boat Show is the top marine event for boating enthusiasts, industry leaders, and world-class exhibitors. As one of the largest and most prestigious boat shows, it showcases the latest in luxury yachts, high-performance boats, cutting-edge marine technology, and innovative accessories. Visitors can explore a vast array of vessels, from powerboats to sailboats, while engaging in hands-on experiences and educational seminars.

Beyond the stunning displays, the event offers an unmatched waterfront lifestyle experience. Attendees can enjoy exciting sea trials, networking opportunities, and immersive activities that celebrate the boating culture. Whether you're a seasoned sailor or a first-time buyer, this show delivers an unforgettable marine adventure.

5. New York International Auto Show 2026

Scheduled Date 03 - 12 April 2026
Location Jacob K. Javits Convention Center, New York, USA
Focus New cars and automobiles

The New York International Auto Show is one of the best car and automotive expos in the USA. It showcases the latest innovations in the automotive industry, featuring cutting-edge technology, futuristic concept cars, and the newest production models from top global manufacturers. Enthusiasts and industry professionals alike can explore a diverse range of vehicles, from high-performance sports cars to eco-friendly electric models, highlighting the future of mobility.

Beyond the impressive vehicle displays, the event offers interactive exhibits, test drives, and expert discussions on automotive trends, sustainability, and advanced driving technology. Whether you're a car enthusiast or simply curious about the industry's future, this auto show promises an exciting experience.

Effective Tips to Make the Most of Your Participation at Motor and Automotive Trade Shows

  • Know your audience: Study attendee profiles of the automotive event you are looking to participate in. Exhibit there only if they are aligned with your business goals.
  • Conduct product demos & displays: Set up an interactive automotive trade show exhibit booth for your auto parts and automotive solutions. Such hands-on engagement boosts retention.
  • Get an engaging booth design: Use eye-catching visuals, branded signage, and tech like AR/VR or touchscreens to make your auto show booth design stand out.
  • Collaborate with Influencers: Inviting auto influencers or YouTubers to the automotive trade show booths generates buzz and boosts online visibility.
  • Run Show-Exclusive Offers: Provide limited-time deals, discounts, or free trials to attract and convert your potential customers faster.
