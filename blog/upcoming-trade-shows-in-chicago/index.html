Top 10 Trade Shows in Chicago 2025-2026

Chicago provides a great environment for trade and commerce, along with a moderate climate. If you are a businessperson looking for the best opportunities to grow your brand reach and expand your market share, exhibiting in Chicago can prove a disruptive strategy. You just need to determine the right trade event and get a visually appealing trade show booth in Chicago for your brand to acquire the maximum benefits from your campaigns.

Explore the ten most anticipated trade fairs in Chicago scheduled in the upcoming days, which we are sharing below. Find the worthy events for participation and start your journey to get to the forefront of your industry.

1. Fabtech Chicago 2025

Date: 08 - 11 September 2025
Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Manufacturing technology and solutions

FABTECH is a distinct trade event that focuses on metal forming, fabricating, welding, and finishing. It showcases the latest manufacturing solutions and technology across fabricating, tube and pipe, welding, finishing, job shop, automation, and stamping/press automation. Here you can easily find top-notch solutions to boost the efficiency of your manufacturing processes.

As an exhibitor at FABTECH, you can amplify your brand value, increase market share, generate valuable leads, and meet world-class suppliers. Participate in this trade show to showcase your equipment and services to highly promising decision makers from around the world. Leverage the opportunity to boost sales and profit, and put your business at the forefront of the industry.

2. Water Environment Federation's Technical Exhibition and Conference (WEFTEC) 2025

Date: 27 September - 01 October 2025
Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Water quality education and training

WEFTEC is a renowned annual technical exhibition and conference for water quality professionals. It brings water quality experts together and offers unparalleled water quality education and training. At this event, you can meet experts, suppliers, and peers in the industry to have important conversations and build valuable relationships to improve water quality.

At WEFTEC, 1,000+ exhibitors showcase the latest water-quality solutions and technologies. You can exchange ideas, knowledge, and experiences with 20,000+ professionals from across the global water sector. Therefore, if you are looking for valuable insights and technology to optimize your water and wastewater treatment operations, this is a must-attend event.

3. ASI Show Chicago 2026

Date: 21 - 23 July 2026
Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Promotional products and services

The Advertising Specialty Institute (ASI) Show is a distinguished trade show dedicated to the advertising and promotional industry. Here, you will find everything you need to take your business to the next level of success. It showcases thousands of promotional products and ideas to help you elevate your brand value in the market.

At the ASI Show, you can also attend educational sessions led by subject-matter experts across the industry to optimize your sales and marketing. You can meet with top professionals and industry leaders to build valuable relationships. Expand your professional network by adding prominent suppliers and distributors. Participate to empower your business to stay ahead in today's competitive environment.

4. PACK Expo International 2026

Date: 18 - 21 October 2026
Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Packaging and processing solutions

The Pack Expo International is a globally-renowned event for manufacturers and suppliers of packaging and processing solutions. More than 2,600 exhibitors present packaging machinery, packaging materials, processing equipment, and related solutions in front of 48,000 potential buyers from 40+ vertical industries at this trade show.

At the Pack Expo, there are endless opportunities for connection, innovation, and sales to grab. You can reach a global audience and have face-to-face interactions with them. You can meet with decision-makers looking for new technologies and solutions to meet their packaging needs. Participate in this expo and acquire remarkable benefits for your business.

5. Automate Show and Conference 2026

Date: 22 - 25 June 2026
Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Robotics and automation

The Automate Show is a prominent robotics and automation event in the USA. It features the latest innovations in robotics, vision, motion control, AI, and automation solutions for a wide range of industries. It brings together buyers, engineers, and executives from all the major industries that have automation-based applications.

This show attracts 50,000+ visitors, including professionals from all experience levels from different industries. It accommodates 1,000+ exhibitors who showcase their latest automation trends and resources. As a conference, it features 200+ expert speakers and 140+ conference sessions. Participate in this incredible conference to learn in-depth insights to get ahead of the curve.

6. Digestive Disease Week (DDW) 2026

Date: 02 - 05 May 2026
Location: Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Digestive diseases and treatments

DDW is a unique digestive health-oriented event that focuses on gastroenterology, hepatology, gastrointestinal surgery, GI endoscopy, and related fields. This event brings together thousands of physicians, researchers, experts, and professionals from the industry under one roof. They gather here to share the latest findings and expert insights with the GI community.

If you are looking to enhance the scientific program, share your findings and ideas with the prominent members of the GI community, conducting a session at DDW is a great way. This event features 400 educational sessions and 4,000+ oral abstract and ePoster presentations. Gain valuable knowledge to offer better care to those living with digestive diseases.

7. The Inspired Home Show 2026

Date: 10 - 12 March 2026
Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Home and housewares products

The Inspired Home Show is a premier home + housewares trade show in the world. Hosted by the International Housewares Association (IHA), this show presents the new products and innovations across the industry. It attracts thousands of global retailers from 23 channels and independent specialty stores seeking home and houseware products and ideas to boost consumers into their stores.

By participating in the Inspired Home Show, you can easily meet unique buyers driven to acquire your products. This show also features the GIA (Global Innovation Awards) program that provides unparalleled recognition to the home & housewares product design excellence. These features make it a perfect event to launch your product and grow your brand.

8. Labelexpo Americas 2026

Date: 15 - 17 September 2026
Location: Donald E. Stephens Convention Center, IL, USA
Focus: Label and package printing solutions

Labelexpo Americas is a prestigious event dedicated to label and package printing operations. It showcases a wide range of label and packaging materials and equipment, including printing presses, prepress and platemaking systems, dies, rollers, inks, coatings, cutters, and so on. You can also find RFID, security solutions, software, and interactive technology for your label and pack printing requirements.

Labelexpo Americas attracts 12,000+ visitors from 70+ countries, who come to explore label and package printing solutions showcased by 450 manufacturers and suppliers. Participate in this event to discover highly efficient solutions and equipment to optimize your package and label printing operations.

9. IEEE PES T&D Conference and Exposition 2026

Date: 04 - 07 May 2026
Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Power and energy transmission and distribution

The IEEE PES T&D Conference and Exposition is an exceptional platform for utilities and systems for electricity infrastructure. It showcases the real-world innovations and cutting-edge technologies that can reshape the future of the grid. You can leverage networking and learning opportunities available here to get and stay ahead of your competitors.

This expo welcomes 800+ exhibitors across the power transmission and distribution sector. It witnesses 15,000+ visitors from 60+ countries. This conference features 100+ educational sessions and 600+ expert speakers. Participate in this event and showcase your products and solutions to a highly interested audience, including decision-makers, and transform the future of your business.

10. ADLM Annual Meeting & Conference 2027

Date: 25 - 29 July 2027
Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL, USA
Focus: Diagnostics and laboratory medicine

The Association for Diagnostics & Laboratory Medicine - ADLM Annual Meeting and Conference is a highly anticipated event for the global laboratory medicine community. It features emerging technologies and products in vitro diagnostics. It provides you with crucial insights about the latest developments in diagnostics and laboratory medicine through 250+ educational opportunities.

ADLM (formerly AACC) features 800+ exhibitors, 200+ new products/services, and a remarkable lineup including expert speakers and scientific sessions. You can connect with the eminent experts and discover cutting-edge technology in clinical chemistry, molecular diagnostics, lab management, translational medicine, mass spectrometry, and other areas in lab medicine. Participate in this event to keep up with the latest changes in the field.

Expert Tips to Maximise Your ROI from Trade Shows in Chicago

Capture Leads Efficiently: Incorporate the latest tech and tools like AI-powered interactive kiosks in your custom trade show display to collect visitors’ contact info.

Network Widely: Attend mixers, panels, and after-hours events to build valuable connections across your industry. Such networking often proves beneficial over time.

Follow Up Promptly: Reach out to leads within 48 hours after the conclusion of the show in a personalised manner. Offer them the exact info they seek without annoying them.

Train Your Staff: Ensure your team at the trade show exhibit booth is knowledgeable, approachable, and energetic. Their behaviour is a key factor for success at trade events.

Track ROI: Evaluate your trade show performance by assessing leads, engagement, and conversions. Find your strengths and weaknesses to refine strategies for future events.
