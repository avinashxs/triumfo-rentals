How to Effectively Use Promotional Items at Your Trade Show Booth?

Trade show giveaway ideas are a strategic lead-generating tactic. They offer a great way to market and promote your product. The question is how useful is the product that you are giving away to your booth visitors. There are many unique and compelling giveaway ideas but you should always look for the one that is cost-effective, brand-oriented, and beneficial for the target audience.

You can leverage the giveaway for a small piece of information, you may ask about contact details, seek feedback, or conduct a survey with your booth visitors. It will create buzz on the floor and more attendees will come to your booth. Now, the question is what should you give them? There are many giveaway ideas that you can use on the trade show floor.

● Key Chain Light

Imagine having your brand name on someone’s key, promoting your brand beyond the show floor. The branded key chain light is an enticing yet useful item you can consider for giveaways. Users can attach it directly to their car keys or other important keys. It’s a cost-effective promotional item that will be in front of everybody's eyes almost every day.

● USB Flash Drive

USB flash drives make a great giveaway for the event. It keeps the user’s digital documents, photos, and music files safe for a long period of time. Personalize them with your logo and brand name. Put the company information on the flash drive and present it to your visitors, making them look back at the information. This works well in the trade shows. What’s a better time for the brand to be visible when you really need a USB flash drive?

● Drawstring Bag

Drawstring bags are the perfect giveaway items for trade shows. This cost-efficient trade show giveaway item can be taken into use right away. Let your visitors walk away with an item that reflects your brand identity. Users can use it everywhere they travel. Imagine showcasing your brand name and logo in front of people coming across while your booth visitors travel with it. 

● Power Banks

A power bank is another remarkable promotional item. They become very useful right at the trade show, as the attendees spend a lot of time roaming on the exhibition floor while their phones’ battery drains significantly. It prevents the attendees from being device-less. A printed power bank with a brand logo is a great way to market your business.
