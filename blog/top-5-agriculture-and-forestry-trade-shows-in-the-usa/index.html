Top 5 Agriculture & Forestry Trade Shows in the USA 2025-2026

Agriculture and forestry have been witnessing remarkable developments and innovations over the last few decades. If you run a business or serve as a prominent decision-maker in an organisation and are looking to keep up with the sector, participating in relevant trade shows can be a great idea. It allows you to gain crucial insights into your industry and also provides opportunities to connect with potential customers and expand your professional network.

Here are the 5 most notable agriculture and forestry trade show booth designs in the USA that are scheduled in the upcoming months. If you are considering participation, exploring cost-effective trade show exhibit rentals in the USA can help you optimize your trade show campaign.

1. AAEA & WAEA Joint Annual Meeting 2025

Scheduled Date 27 - 29 July 2025
Location Sheraton Denver Downtown Hotel, Denver, USA

The AAEA & WAEA Joint Annual Meeting brings together professionals, researchers, and students from agricultural and applied economics for an enriching exchange of ideas and insights. This gathering offers a dynamic platform to showcase the latest research, policy discussions, and industry trends shaping the future of agriculture, food systems, and rural economies. You can engage in thought-provoking sessions, network with leading experts, and participate in discussions that drive innovation and practical solutions.

With a diverse range of presentations, workshops, and panel discussions, this joint meeting helps to foster collaboration among academics, policymakers, and industry professionals. Whether sharing groundbreaking research or discovering new opportunities for professional growth, you can find valuable connections and knowledge that advance your work in agricultural and applied economics by taking part in this joint AAEA & WAEA meeting.

2. Minnesota Farmfest 2025

Scheduled Date 05 - 07 August 2025
Location Redwood County, Minnesota, USA

Minnesota Farmfest is an exclusive agricultural event for farmers, industry leaders, and innovators. More than 500 exhibitors and vendors gather here to showcase the latest advancements in farming technology, equipment, and sustainable practices. With a strong focus on education and networking, the event features expert-led discussions that highlight the future of agriculture.

At this farmfest, you can explore products and services tailored to modern farming needs, from precision agriculture tools to livestock management solutions. Whether you are looking to connect with industry experts or discover cutting-edge farming techniques, this show offers a dynamic platform to learn, engage, and grow within the agricultural community.

3. Farm Progress Show 2025

Scheduled Date 26 - 28 August 2025
Location 4275 E Mound Rd, Decatur, IL, USA

The Farm Progress Show is the nation’s largest outdoor farm event, gathering farmers, agribusiness professionals, and industry leaders for an immersive experience in the latest agricultural advancements. This show is an excellent platform to showcase cutting-edge farming equipment, precision technology, and innovative solutions designed to improve efficiency and sustainability.

Live field demonstrations at this show offer a hands-on look at modern techniques in action, providing valuable insights for growers of all sizes. Along with equipment and technology, the show is a hub for networking and education. Farmers can connect with experts, attend informative sessions, and discover new products to enhance their operations. It’s a must-visit event for anyone passionate about agriculture’s future.

4. Plant & Animal Genome Conference (PAG) 2026

Scheduled Date 09 - 14 January 2026
Location Town & Country Resort & Convention Center, San Diego, CA, USA

The Plant & Animal Genome Conference (PAG) is the leading global event for advancing genetic research in agriculture and biodiversity. It brings together top scientists, industry experts, and innovators. This event showcases the latest breakthroughs in genome analysis, gene editing, and bioinformatics. You can gain valuable insights into them through expert-led presentations, workshops. It offers remarkable networking opportunities, fostering collaboration across academia and industry.

With a strong focus on plant and animal genetics, PAG provides a space for discussions on improving crop resilience, livestock health, and sustainable agriculture. The conference showcases emerging technologies, paving the way for innovations that shape the future of genomics.

5. World Ag Expo 2026

Scheduled Date 10 - 12 February 2026
Location International Agri-Center, Tulare County, USA

The World Ag Expo is the ultimate gathering for agriculture professionals, innovators, and industry leaders from across the globe. As the largest annual agricultural trade show, it showcases cutting-edge technology, advanced machinery, and groundbreaking solutions that drive the future of farming. You can explore what hundreds of exhibitors showcase, attend expert-led seminars, and connect with key players from the global agricultural landscape.

From precision farming to sustainable practices, the event covers every aspect of modern agriculture. Whether you are a grower, rancher, or agribusiness professional, the World Ag Expo offers valuable insights, networking opportunities, and the latest industry advancements to help you get and stay at the forefront of the industry.

Effective Tips to Gain Visibility at Agriculture & Forestry Trade Shows in the USA

  • Know Your Audience: Research the event attendees and tailor your messaging to address farmers, agronomists, or forestry professionals.
  • Get an Attractive Booth: Use eye-catching visuals, live demos, or interactive displays to make your booth engaging and memorable.
  • Showcase Real Solutions: Highlight the benefits of your products with practical case studies or testimonials relevant to the agricultural operations.
  • Train Your Staff: Ensure your team is well-versed in product knowledge and capable of addressing industry-specific questions confidently.
  • Follow Up Promptly: Send thank-you notes, product information, or proposals shortly after the event to maintain momentum.
