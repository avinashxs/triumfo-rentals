Top 6 Trade Shows in New York City 2025-2026

New York City has always surprised its visitors with plenty of attractions, and when it comes to business, things become more exciting. It hosts various internationally-renowned trade shows annually for different industries, providing numerous business opportunities to participating businesses. Exhibiting at these events can significantly transform your business growth rate.

If you are also looking for business opportunities and expanding your market, this blog is for you. Here, we will tell you about various leading trade shows in New York City that you can consider participating in. We also suggest that you showcase your brand in captivating and engaging trade show booth rentals in New York to stand out on the exhibition floor.

Here are 6 major exhibitions and trade shows in New York that offer the best opportunities:

1. GNYDM New York 2025

Dates 30 November - 03 December 2025
Venue Jacob K. Javits Convention Center, New York City, NY, USA
Focus Latest innovations and education in the dental industry

The Greater New York Dental Meeting (GNYDM) is a highly anticipated event for professionals in the dental field. It offers various opportunities to transform their career, such as learning about new practices, networking, expansion, and much more. It presents learning opportunities through live patient demonstrations, lectures, and hands-on workshops.

Exhibitors, including top companies in the dental industry, at this event will showcase digital solutions and the latest equipment that can optimize dental patient care. GNYDM also features seminars led by experts that focus on dental advancements. Participate in this event to meet, learn, and collaborate with dental experts for your professional growth.

2. Footwear Show New York Expo (FSNYE) 2025

Dates 02 - 04 December 2025
Venue Park Lane Hotel New York, NY, USA
Focus Connections, innovations, and education in the footwear industry

Footwear Show New York Expo (FSNYE) is one of the leading B2B footwear trade shows in the USA. It brings together key retailers, wholesalers, distributors, and professionals across the footwear industry under one roof. As an exhibiting company at this footwear event, you can connect with both existing and potential customers to expand your market reach.

At FSNYE, connect and collaborate with renowned brands, suppliers, and influencers to help grow your business. It also features expert sessions where you can gain incredible insights into consumer preferences, latest trends, and the competitive landscape. Participate in this event to boost brand awareness, enhance credibility, and grab sales opportunities.

3. NRF Retail's Big Show

Dates 11-13 January 2026
Venue Jacob K. Javits Convention Center, New York City, NY, USA
Focus Connections, education, and the latest solutions in the retail industry

National Retail Federation (NRF) Retail's Big Show is a prominent expo for the retail industry. It attracts industry leaders, brands, entrepreneurs, suppliers, experts, investors, and SMEs in the retail sector under one roof. The retail categories of this event are food service operations, F&B technology, AI, machine learning, facial recognition, and many more. There will be great opportunities for both merchandisers and retail buyers.

NRF Retail's Big Show brings vendors and retailers together from 90+ nations and offers unparalleled educational and networking opportunities. At this trade show, there will be 450+ speakers, 170+ education sessions, 40,000+ attendees, and 1,000+ exhibitors. If you are seeking actionable insights about AI and other emerging technologies for your business strategies, this is a perfect expo for you.

4. Toy Fair New York

Dates 14 - 17 February 2026
Venue Jacob K. Javits Convention Center, New York City, NY, USA
Focus Latest products and deep insights into the toy and play industry

Toy Fair New York is a unique trade show dedicated to toys, games, and immersive entertainment for kids. It attracts buyers, manufacturers, retailers, inventors, and top-level professionals from around the world. This fair is a brilliant platform to showcase the latest innovations and explore the emerging trends across the kids’ play and entertainment industry.

Toy Fair NY carries unlimited potential for exhibiting companies. Here you can showcase innovative products, connect with industry leaders, and gain insights that redefine the future of your business. Capture extensive attention from the media personnel and potential buyers and drive your business to the forefront of your industry.

5. InsurTech Spring Conference 2026

Dates 30-31 March 2026
Venue Chelsea Piers, Pier 60, New York, NY, USA
Focus Latest tech and business models in the insurance landscape

InsurTech Spring Conference is a leading trade event that provides numerous business opportunities for professionals in the insurance industry. Several industry leaders, experts, decision-makers, renowned companies, and professionals attend this trade show in New York. It is a perfect platform with opportunities for traditional insurers, InsurTechs, investors, start-ups, and other solution providers to grow and boost their profits.

Participate in this event to connect with more than 900 industry leaders, with >80% of them at VP or higher positions. 99+ eminent speakers share valuable knowledge and insights over two major segments of insurance topics, i.e, Property & Casualty (P&C), and Life & Health (L&H). You must participate in this conference to transform the future of your business.

6. ICSC NEW YORK 2026

Dates 14-15 December 2026
Venue Jacob K. Javits Convention Center, New York City, NY, USA
Focus Innovations in the marketplace industry

The International Council of Shopping Centers (ICSC) New York Convention is a perfect platform for negotiators, money-makers, and dealmakers of the marketplace industry to gain numerous business advantages. Experts and top professionals of the industry gather here to discuss and share global insights and data, next-generation leadership, professional growth, innovation, and industry growth.

ICSC Convention 2026 helps you connect with various industry leaders, allowing you to expand your professional network. Gain a deep insight into the various strategies and business practices used by the experts to stay ahead in the industry. Participate in this convention and put your business on the fast track to growth.

Success Tips for Exhibiting at a New York Trade Show

Plan Early: New York exhibition venues book up quickly since they have global demand. Secure your space and logistics well in advance.

Understand Your Audience: Tailor your messaging for a diverse and fast-paced trade show audience at New York events.

Design for Impact: Use bold graphics, clean layouts, and strategic lighting in a 10x20 booth or a bigger one to grab attention.

Engage Actively: Offer in-depth product demos, top-tier samples, or experiences to attract and engage visitors.

Leverage Local Media: Promote your brand’s presence at New York trade shows through press and social channels.
