Top Trade Shows in USA 2025-2026

America is the melting pot of nationalities and businesses. The country is driven by its natural-born citizens and the ones who come to the country for entrepreneurial purposes. From old companies like Ford Motor to the newest businesses like Meta, the US has reshaped the way the world operates.

Doing business and attending trade shows in the USA offers multiple advantages. The exhibitions in the USA offer excellent space for entrepreneurship and provide a dynamic and profitable business environment. The expos of the USA give you direct access to thousands of potential customers and benefits with networking opportunities.

Here we have suggested the best trade shows in 2025 - 2026 in the USA that are best for launching or boosting the business:

EDS Leadership Summit

Dates May 19–May 23, 2025
Venue Las Vegas, United States 

EDS Leadership Summit 2025 is a leadership event that is joined by movers, the brightest minds, and shakers from the electronic components industry. The event offers an energized, pro­gres­sive atmos­phere to build business connections with top decision-makers. The event is the best place to discuss new opportunities and rebuild the older ones. The exhibition is starting from May 19–May 23, 2025, in Las Vegas, Us.

WVC Las Vegas

Dates February 15-18, 2026
Venue Las Vegas, United States

WVC 2026 event is celebrating 95 years of the veterinary industry from February 15-18, 2026, in Las Vegas, USA. The expo is the largest veterinary conference that allows in-person meetings with 12000+ participants and 400+ exhibitors.

The WVC Las Vegas event is dedicated to the constant improvements in vaccines related to animals. The event will present multiple seminars and hundreds of hours of content on the latest solutions for animal medications, surgery, anesthetics, and other practice management.

VIVE Los Angeles

Dates February 22- 25, 2026
Venue Los Angeles, United States

VIVE 2026 is a business event for the healthcare industry going to take place for 4 days from February 22 - 25, 2026, in Los Angeles, CA. The expo is dedicated to delivering unforgettable experiences related to IT innovation in healthcare and transformation in business.

VIVE Los Angeles event will be joined by senior business leaders and thus offer the best prospects to make connections in an engaging environment. The upcoming trade show booth design offers curated connections with industry peers, and it will focus on digital health solutions.

Palm Beach International Boat Show

Dates March 25-29, 2026
Venue Palm Beach, United States

Palm Beach International Boat Show is a 4-day event that highlights the yacht industry held from March 25-29, 2026, in Palm Beach, USA. The expo will attract around $1.05B in capital and will be attended by 55000+ boat show attendees, 600+ exhibiting brands, and 800+ boats on display. The expo will feature more than $1.2 billion worth of yachts and other related products for thousands of attendees.

IWCE Orlando

Dates 16-19 March 2026
Venue Orlando, United States

IWCE 2026 is an International Wireless Communications Expo that connects people and technologies on a single platform. The expo supports transportation, government agencies, public safety professionals, and the healthcare industry.

The event is power-packed with the brightest minds and decision-makers who will come together to make significant changes in the industry. The IWCE event will focus on topics like critical communication, infrastructure, security, policy, governance, and emerging technology.

MODEX Atlanta

Dates April 13-16, 2026
Venue Atlanta, United States

The Modex 2026 event will be taking place from April 13-16, 2026, at the Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, USA. Here, exhibitors will showcase their software technology to supply chain buyers and get an edge in the market.

This expo is attended by 91% of exhibitors to keep abreast of their companies ' new industry trends and issues. 90% of the audience joined the show floor to watch out for new products. About 83% of exhibitors get a high return on investment, and 66% of them pass in making good networks with suppliers.

Hope this blog has helped you to know the major upcoming events in the USA. Taking part in these shows could be a game-changer for your business. It is best to book a trade show exhibit booth provider in the USA so that your products are highlighted on the show floor.

The NAMA Show Las Vegas

Dates April 22-24, 2026
Venue Las Vegas, United States

The NAMA Show 2026 is taking place for place from April 22-24, 2026, in the US. The event will present new ideas, possibilities, and technologies in the convenience sector. About 300+ exhibitors will present their 100+ products related to vending, kiosks, micro markets, unattended retail, self-services, coffee service, and pantry.

Border Security Expo

Dates May 5-6, 2026
Venue Phoenix, United States

Border Security Expo Every year, federal, local, state, and international governments come together to get expert insights and the latest security technology at the Border Security Expo. The event will be held from May 5-6, 2026, in Phoenix, US.

The event will present products and services related to Surveillance Cameras, Systems Integrators, Guards & Bomb Sniffing Dogs, Electronic Identification Systems, Intelligence Sharing, Wireless Communications, Voice Recognition Systems, Biometrics Products, Control Monitors, Motion Detectors, and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles, etc.

DDW San Diego

Dates May 2–5, 2026
Venue Chicago, IL, United States

DDW 2026 is the world's premier event for experts belonging to hepatology, GI endoscopy, gastroenterology, gastrointestinal surgery, and other professionals to provide solutions for digestive diseases. The expo will be taking place May 2–5, 2026, in Chicago, IL. The expo will bring about 10000+ physicians together on a single platform to discover and get insights about the solutions to treat digestive diseases.

Techtextil North America

Dates 4 -6 August, 2026
Venue Raleigh, United States

The Techtextil North America show is for the technical textile industry. The exhibition will present the latest research and developments in raw materials and manufacturing processes. The upcoming trade show encourages ideas and collaborations with industry peers in an engaging environment. Take part in the Techtextil North America show from 4 -6 August 2026 at the Raleigh Convention Center, USA.
