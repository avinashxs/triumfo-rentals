Top 5 Logistics & Transportation Trade Shows in the USA

Logistics and transportation are integral to almost every industry. This sector holds enormous opportunities and vast potential for the future. If you are a professional or run a business in this field and are seeking ways to expand your market reach, participating in relevant trade shows is an apt strategy.

Here are the 5 most prominent transportation and logistics events scheduled across the USA in the coming months. Choose the best ones among them and showcase your business effectively with an engaging trade show exhibit booth in the USA.

1. Logistics Development Forum 2025

Scheduled Date 28 - 30 July 2025
Location Park Hyatt Beaver Creek Resort and Spa, Beaver Creek, CO, USA
Location Logistical processes and supply chain management

The Logistics Development Forum is a leading logistics trade show that, in collaboration with industry leaders, decision-makers, and innovators, shapes the future of logistics and supply chain management. This exclusive forum fosters high-level discussions, strategic networking, and collaboration between experts seeking to optimize operations, integrate new technologies, and address the challenges in the industry.

With a focus on cutting-edge solutions and best practices, you can engage in insightful sessions, interactive workshops, and one-on-one meetings tailored to drive meaningful business growth. The trade show for logistics companies offers a unique opportunity to connect with key stakeholders and gain valuable insights into the latest trends shaping global logistics.

2. Fort Lauderdale International Boat Show (FLIBS) 2025

Scheduled Date 29 October - 02 November 2025
Location Seven locations across Fort Lauderdale, FL, USA
Focus Boats, yachts, and related solutions

The Fort Lauderdale International Boat Show (FLIBS) is the world’s largest in-water boat show, aligning marine enthusiasts, industry specialists, and luxury lifestyle seekers. Showcasing an impressive fleet of yachts, superyachts, and cutting-edge marine technology, FLIBS is one of the top destinations for discovering the latest innovations in boating. From high-performance sport boats to extravagant megayachts, the event offers something for every level of boating enthusiast.

Beyond the stunning vessels, FLIBS features exclusive exhibits, live demonstrations, and networking opportunities with top yacht builders, designers, and brokers. With luxury experiences, marine accessories, and waterfront entertainment, it’s a must-visit trade for anyone passionate about the nautical world.

3. Transportation Research Board (TRB) Annual Meeting 2026

Scheduled Date 11 - 15 January 2026
Location Walter E. Washington Convention Center, Washington, D.C., USA
Focus Sessions and workshops on all transportation modes

The Transportation Research Board (TRB) Annual Meeting brings together transportation experts, researchers, and policymakers to explore the latest advancements in the field. With a focus on innovation, sustainability, and emerging technologies, the transportation trade show features thousands of presentations, interactive sessions, and networking opportunities designed to shape the future of transportation.

You can discover valuable insights into cutting-edge research, policy discussions, and best practices across all modes of transportation. Whether you are looking to share knowledge, discover new trends, or connect with industry leaders, this premier event offers a dynamic platform for meaningful collaboration and learning.

4. CMA Shipping Expo & Conference 2026

Scheduled Date March 10 - 12, 2026
Location Hilton Stamford Hotel & Executive Meeting Center, Stamford, CT, USA
Focus Shipping and logistics solutions

The CMA Shipping Expo & Conference is an exclusive event that attracts maritime professionals, industry leaders, and innovators from around the globe. As a renowned trade show for transportation companies, it offers invaluable networking opportunities, insightful panel discussions, and expert-led sessions covering the latest trends, challenges, and advancements in the shipping industry. You can explore cutting-edge solutions, engage with key decision-makers, and gain critical insights into the future of global trade and maritime operations.

With an expansive exhibition floor showcasing the latest technologies and services, CMA Shipping provides a platform for businesses to connect, collaborate, and stay ahead in an evolving industry. Whether you are a shipowner, supplier, or industry stakeholder, this event offers an essential opportunity to drive growth and innovation.

5. Mid-America Trucking Show (MATS) 2026

Scheduled Date March 26 - 28, 2026
Location Kentucky Exposition Center, Louisville, KY, USA
Focus Technology, solutions, and education for trucking businesses

The Mid-America Trucking Show (MATS) is the largest annual trucking event in North America, connecting industry specialists, manufacturers, and enthusiasts for an immersive experience. With a massive exhibition space featuring the latest trucks, trailers, parts, and services, MATS is the ultimate trade show for trucking companies to network, learn, and discover cutting-edge innovations in the industry.

Beyond the exhibits, you can attend educational seminars, hands-on demonstrations, and special events designed to celebrate and support the trucking community. Whether you are a fleet owner, driver, or industry supplier, MATS provides valuable insights, business opportunities, and a firsthand look at the future of trucking.

Expert Tips to Easily Achieve Your Exhibition Goals at the Logistics Exhibition

  • Define Clear Objectives: Know whether you want lead generation, brand awareness, partnerships, or service promotion by participating in the logistics expo.
  • Pre-Show Marketing: Use email campaigns, social media, and industry publications before the logistics conference commences to drive traffic to your booth.
  • Showcase Unique Strengths: Highlight what sets your fleet, tech, or service apart from competitors. Use visuals, videos, and real-time tracking demos at the logistics show.
  • Create an Inviting Booth: Use a smartly designed exhibition stand at the logistics fair that reflects movement, efficiency, and reliability.
