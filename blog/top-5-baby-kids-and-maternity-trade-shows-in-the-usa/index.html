Top 5 Baby Kids & Maternity Trade Shows in the USA in 2026

When it comes to kids, pregnancy, or maternity products, innovation and quality define the market. For businesses dealing in them, trade events offer incredible opportunities to showcase innovative products, connect with retailers, and stay informed about market trends. From baby care essentials to innovative toys and maternity wear, these events attract buyers, manufacturers, and industry professionals alike.

You can take your business to the next level by participating in such trade events. To make a lasting impression, an appealing trade show exhibit booth is essential. An eye-catching booth boosts engagement and reinforces your brand. Take your first step towards success by choosing the right events to participate in.

Here is a list of the top 5 baby, kids, and maternity trade shows in the USA to start with.

1. KidsWorld Market 2026

Dates: 20 - 23 January 2026
Location: Dallas Market Center, Dallas, TX, USA
Focus: Baby & kids apparel and accessories

KidsWorld Market is a distinct trade fair dedicated to infant and children’s products. It showcases a huge collection of infant and children’s apparel, accessories, toys, gifts, footwear, nursery décor items, and more.

If you deal in the children’s products mentioned above and are looking for highly promising buyers, you can’t miss this event.

2. Playtime & Kid’s Hub New York 2026

Dates: 08–10 February 2026
Location: Metropolitan Pavilion, New York City, NY, USA
Focus: Children's fashion & maternity apparel

Playtime & Kid’s Hub New York is one of the leading events in the kids’ fashion and lifestyle industry. It focuses on children's fashion, accessories, and maternity wear, catering to a more boutique-style market.

This event draws a curated audience of boutique retailers, offering a great platform for high-quality and trendy product lines. It is a must-attend trade fair for businesses in the kids’ fashion industry.

3. Toy Fair New York 2026

Dates: 14 – 17 February 2026
Location: Jacob K. Javits Center, New York, NY, USA
Focus: Toy products and the global play community

Toy Fair (New York) is a highly anticipated event for the global play community. It features thousands of toys, games, and immersive entertainment activities. This event attracts buyers, retailers, manufacturers, inventors, and entertainment executives worldwide.

The North American International Toy Fair offers a platform to learn about emerging trends and connect with innovators across the industry.

4. Kidscreen Summit 2026

Dates: 22 - 25 February 2026
Location: Marriott Marquis San Diego Marina, San Diego, CA, USA
Focus: Children's media & Education products

Kidscreen Summit is primarily a media event and offers a platform for brands in the children’s entertainment and education industries. 1,800+ delegates from 50+ countries attend this trade event.

If your brand intersects with children’s education or media, this summit is a unique opportunity to form partnerships and explore creative collaborations.

5. ABC Kids Expo 2026

Dates: 13 – 15 May 2026
Location: Mandalay Bay Resort & Convention Center, Las Vegas, NV, USA
Focus: Baby gear, toys & furniture

ABC Kids Expo is one of the largest baby and kids' product trade shows in the USA. It showcases a huge collection of baby gear, toys, apparel, gifts, and more. This trade fair attracts exhibitors and attendees from across the globe.

This event offers unparalleled opportunities to network with retailers, discover innovative products, and gain insights into trends in children's products.

Tips for Exhibiting Successfully

Focus on Presentation: Use creative and functional booth designs to highlight your products effectively.

Engage Visitors: Offer product demonstrations or sampling to draw in potential buyers and partners.

Leverage Visual Storytelling: Create a booth that tells your brand's story and connects emotionally with attendees.

Plan Strategically: Secure meetings with essential buyers in advance to maximize the ROI of your participation.

Conclusion

The top baby, kids, and maternity trade shows in the USA are more than just events; they are gateways to growth and visibility for brands in the industry. These trade shows allow you to showcase your products, network with key players, and gain a competitive edge.

To make your presence impactful, invest in a well-designed custom trade show display that resonates with your target audience.
