Top 10 Trade Shows in Anaheim in 2025-2026

Choosing the right trade show requires thorough research when planning to exhibit. In the USA, numerous global trade shows are held annually, particularly in California. Anaheim, home to Disneyland, is a leading destination in this state for international trade shows and conventions.

This Orange County city attracts thousands of tourists and visitors due to its world-class rides, restaurants, hotels, and shops. With its vibrant atmosphere, Anaheim could be the ideal location for your next trade show campaign. Determine apt trade events for your business, get enticing trade show booth rentals, and elevate your brand recognition value to new heights.

Here are the top ten trade shows in Anaheim scheduled in 2025-2026 worth participating in:

1. Anaheim Electronics & Manufacturing Show

Date 24 - 25 September 2025
Venue Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Latest products and innovations in the electronics industry

The Anaheim Electronics & Manufacturing Show is a comprehensive exposition and seminar program that represents the electronics manufacturing sector. This dynamic expo showcases the latest advancements in automation, circuit design, 3D printing, and precision engineering, offering a platform for networking, collaboration, and hands-on demonstrations of cutting-edge technologies.

At AEMS, you can explore a diverse range of electronics products and innovations, attend expert-led seminars, and gain valuable insights into emerging trends shaping the future of electronics manufacturing. Whether you are seeking new suppliers, innovative solutions, or industry connections, this event is an essential destination for businesses looking to expand their market reach.

2. Global Produce & Floral Show 2025

Date 16 - 18 October 2025
Venue Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Latest products and innovations in the electronics industry

The Global Produce & Floral Show is the top event for the fresh produce and floral industry. It brings together professionals, suppliers, buyers, and experts from around the world. This dynamic trade show offers unparalleled opportunities for networking, business growth, and discovering the latest innovations in fresh produce, floral products, and supply chain solutions.

At this floral show, you can explore cutting-edge products, services, and technologies shaping the future of the industry. Beyond those exhibits, the event hosts insightful educational sessions, keynote presentations, and panel discussions led by industry leaders. It’s the ultimate platform for forging valuable connections, gaining market insights, and keeping up with the evolving produce and floral landscape.

3. The NAMM Show 2026

Date 20 - 24 January 2026
Venue Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Music, sound, and entertainment technology

The NAMM Show is the ultimate gathering for professionals and enthusiasts in the music, sound, and entertainment technology industries. As one of the largest trade shows of its kind, it brings together top brands, groundbreaking innovations, and world-class educational sessions. It showcases the latest musical instruments, recording gear, and audio technology while connecting with industry leaders, artists, and influencers.

With live performances, hands-on demos, and networking opportunities, the event is a hub of creativity and collaboration. Whether you are a musician, producer, retailer, or content creator, this show offers an unmatched experience to discover trends, gain insights, and shape the future of music.

4. MD&M West 2026

Date 03 - 05 February 2026
Venue Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus MedTech and its advanced manufacturing

MD&M West is the premier event for medical device manufacturing, bringing together industry leaders, engineers, and innovators to explore the latest advancements in MedTech. At this event, you get the opportunity to connect with top suppliers, discover cutting-edge technologies, and gain insights from expert-led sessions covering key industry trends.

From product development to regulatory updates, this event provides a comprehensive platform for professionals looking to enhance their knowledge and expand their networks. With a dynamic expo floor featuring groundbreaking solutions and live demonstrations, MD&M West is the ultimate destination for those who want to improve the future of medical manufacturing.

5. Natural Products Expo West 2026

Date 03 - 06 March 2026
Venue Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Natural products for sustainability and wellness

Natural Products Expo West is an outstanding event for the natural, organic, and healthy products industry. It brings together leading brands, retailers, distributors, and industry leaders under one roof. This dynamic trade show showcases the latest innovations in food, beverages, supplements, beauty, and eco-friendly living.

At NPE West, you can explore a diverse range of natural products, engage in networking opportunities, and gain valuable insights from expert-led educational sessions. With a focus on sustainability and wellness, the event showcases cutting-edge trends shaping the future of conscious consumerism. Whether you are seeking emerging brands or industry expertise, this expo is the ultimate destination for growth and inspiration.

6. IPC Apex Expo 2026

Date 13 -19 March 2026
Venue Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Research and collaboration across the electronics industry

The IPC APEX EXPO is a notable event for the electronics manufacturing industry. It attracts innovators, engineers, and business leaders from around the world. This globally recognized conference features cutting-edge technical sessions, professional development courses, and a world-class exhibition showcasing the latest advancements in PCB design, manufacturing, and assembly.

At IPC APEX EXPO, you get the opportunity to explore emerging technologies, network with industry experts, and gain valuable insights into market trends shaping the future of electronics. Participate in this electronics expo and secure promising opportunities to drive your brand to new heights of recognition.

7. California HR Conference 2026 (CAHR26)

Date 11 -13 May 2026
Venue Hilton Anaheim, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus HR practices and insights

California HR Conference is a premier event for HR professionals across various industries. It provides you with optimal knowledge and tools through engaging keynotes, in-depth sessions, and practical strategies to excel in your role as HR in your organization. This conference features educational sessions to help you understand complex regulations and the legal landscape.

CAHR Conference witnesses 1200+ attendees, out of which 76% are positioned as managers or higher-level professionals in their respective organizations. This event features educational sessions on 12 distinct lines, including leadership, workplace culture, and innovation. Participate in this conference to gain valuable insights to efficiently accomplish your HR tasks.

8. The Art and Science of Dentistry 2026

Date 14 -16 May 2026
Venue Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Educational sessions, workshops, and innovations in dentistry

The Art and Science of Dentistry by the California Dental Association (CDA) is a highly anticipated event for dental care professionals. It showcases the latest advancements in dental tools and services presented by 450+ exhibitors. This event also features 180+ educational sessions and hands-on workshops led by 130+ expert speakers.

This dental show attracts thousands of professionals, allowing you to build meaningful relationships and learn from the finest experts in the field. This event also features fun events and live entertainment activities. If you are looking for promising opportunities to gain knowledge and expand your network across the dental care field, this is a must-attend event.

9. The Western Foot & Ankle Conference 2026

Date 25 - 28 June 2026
Venue Disneyland® Hotel & Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Podiatric medical education and practices

The Western Foot & Ankle Conference brings together leading podiatrists, surgeons, and healthcare professionals for an unparalleled educational and networking experience. Featuring cutting-edge lectures, hands-on workshops, and industry exhibits, this remarkable event covers the latest advancements in podiatric medicine, surgery, and patient care. You can gain valuable knowledge while engaging with experts and peers in a dynamic learning environment.

The Western Conference offers a diverse program tailored to current trends and challenges in foot and ankle health. It is designed for professionals seeking to enhance their clinical skills and expand their knowledge. Don't miss this opportunity to get and stay ahead in the field.

10. ADLM Annual Meeting and Clinical Lab Expo 2026

Date 26 - 30 July 2026
Venue Anaheim Convention Center, Anaheim, CA, USA
Focus Diagnostics and laboratory medicine

ADLM Annual Meeting & Clinical Lab Expo (formerly AACC) is a premier event for laboratory medicine professionals. It attracts the experts and top professionals associated with clinical chemistry, mass spectrometry, molecular diagnostics, lab management, translational medicine, and other related areas to laboratory medicine sciences. Around 900 exhibitors participate in this expo, and 200+ new products are launched at this event.

This medicine expo features a comprehensive conference program with 250+ educational sessions. It offers learning opportunities about the latest and significant changes in the field. Learn about cutting-edge technology and the latest developments in the field through scientific sessions, plenaries, and roundtables, and keep up with this ever-evolving field.

Key Tips for Successful Exhibiting at a Trade Show in Anaheim

Plan Early: Secure your booth space, book accommodations, and hire shipping service providers well in advance to avoid last-minute costs.

Know the Audience: Research attendee profiles to tailor your messaging and offerings.

Create a Visual Impact: Use bold graphics, clear branding, and digital displays in your custom trade show display to attract attention.

Train Your Team: Ensure staff are knowledgeable, engaging, and aligned with your objectives. An efficient team can produce incredible results even with a 10x10 booth.

Follow Up Fast: Contact leads promptly after the show to convert interest into business.
