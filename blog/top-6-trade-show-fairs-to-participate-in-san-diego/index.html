Top 6 Trade Shows to Participate in San Diego 2025-2026

Countless trade shows and exhibitions are organised every year in various cities in the USA. You must choose only the most suitable events to promote your products and connect with potential customers. This may be challenging due to the vast array of choices, especially in a city like San Diego, one of the most popular locations in the US for expos and conferences.

Many exhibiting companies, from start-ups and seasoned exhibitors, descend upon San Diego to present their finest selves and compete to get to the forefront of their industry. Determine and participate in the trade events to gain benefits for your business. You must get a captivating trade show display in San Diego to make your brand presence unforgettable.

Let us help you improve your business growth by telling you about some remarkable trade fairs happening in San Diego. Here are the upcoming trade fairs you can consider to participate in 2025-2026:

1. San Diego Comic-Con International 2025

Dates: 24 - 27 July 2025
Venue: San Diego Convention Center, San Diego, CA, USA
Focus: Comic book convention and multi-genre entertainment event

San Diego Comic-Con is a distinguished comic-book convention. This multi-genre entertainment and comic con attracts 500 exhibitors and 50,000+ attendees, including dignitaries, scholars, and comic industry professionals worldwide.

Comic-Con celebrates comics and associated popular art forms such as movies, television, gaming, and interactive multimedia. It showcases extensive merchandise representing all fandoms, along with featuring cosplay galore, exclusive previews, and presentations.

2. NACDS Total Store Expo (TSE) 2025

Dates: 23 - 25 August 2025
Venue: San Diego Convention Center, San Diego, CA, USA
Focus: Health and wellness products suppliers and retailers

The NACDS Total Store Expo (TSE) is a highly anticipated event for the health and wellness industry. It attracts a huge number of retailers and suppliers who showcase unique and innovative products, technology, and services.

The TSE provides networking opportunities with professionals and decision-makers across various categories, such as consumer packaged goods (CPG), over-the-counter (OTC), supply chain, beauty, logistics technology, asset protection, and so on.

3. PepTalk 2026

Dates: 19 - 22 Jan 2026
Venue: Hilton Bayfront, San Diego, CA, USA
Focus: Biotherapeutic discovery and development

The PepTalk, aka Protein Science and Production Week, is a hybrid event dedicated to bio-therapeutic discovery and development. It attracts thousands of attendees, including scientists, trailblazers, and industry leaders. It features around a hundred premier exhibitors who showcase the latest advancements in protein science.

This event features an extensive conference program and training seminars about protein expression, production platforms, analytical methods, lab automation for higher throughput, antibody discovery and development, targeted therapies, and drug delivery systems. Participate in PepTalk to learn about the cutting-edge research and practical solutions in protein science.

4. Child and Family Maltreatment Conference 2026

Dates: 24 - 29 Jan 2026
Venue: 500 Hotel Circle North, San Diego, CA
Focus: Child and family maltreatment

Sponsored by Chadwick Center for Children and Families at Rady Children’s Hospital-San Diego, the Child and Family Maltreatment Conference primarily focuses on child and family maltreatment around the USA. It features virtual posters and lighting presentations by students and professionals in the early stages of their careers (upto five years).

This conference offers an opportunity for collaboration and multidisciplinary interactions to enhance your professional skills and knowledge to recognize, investigate, and treat all forms of child and family maltreatment using best practices.

5. Intersolar & Energy Storage North America 2026

Dates: 18 - 20 February, 2026
Venue: San Diego Convention Center in San Diego, CA
Focus: Solar, energy storage, and EV charging infrastructure

Intersolar & Energy Storage (North America) is a prestigious exhibition dedicated to solar, storage, EV charging infrastructure, and manufacturing. It attracts thousands of professionals associated with services like installation, business development, engineering, program/project management, sales, and related processes.

The IESNA is a remarkable platform to source quality products, gain crucial industry insights, and make impactful business connections. Boost your brand reach among leaders, innovators, and clean energy enthusiasts to get to the forefront of the industry.

6. Del Mar Electronics & Manufacturing Show (DMEMS) 2026

Dates: 22 - 23 April 2026
Venue: Del Mar Fairgrounds in San Diego
Focus: Home decor products and electronics solutions

Del Mar Electronics & Manufacturing Show, or DMEMS, is a premier design & manufacturing show for the electronics, medical, and biotech industries. It is an excellent platform to showcase electronic components, IC products and services, fabrication products & services, instrumentation equipment, engineering software & services, 3D printers and scanners, IoT, and much more. If you are dealing in home decor, electronics, or medical and biotech technology and are looking for promising business opportunities, you must participate in this event.
