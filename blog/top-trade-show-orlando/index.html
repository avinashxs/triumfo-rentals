Top 7 Trade Shows in Orlando in 2025-2026

Orlando is one of the most visited cities in the world, primarily due to its tourism industry. It is also a major industrial and technological center in the US and hosts numerous trade shows annually. Exhibiting at these trade fairs can be very beneficial for your business. However, you must get an engaging trade show booth in Orlando to showcase your brand.

If you want to exhibit there and learn about the most prominent upcoming business events, you don’t need to look further. Here is the list of prominent trade fairs to be held in Orlando in 2025 - 2026.

1. The Clean Show 2025

Dates 23 - 26 August 2025
Venue Orange County Convention Center, Orlando, FL, USA
Focus Products for laundering, dry cleaning, and textile care services

The Clean Show is a premier trade show for textile cleaning and care professionals. It showcases the latest innovations, supplies, and equipment for laundering, dry cleaning, and textile care services. This event attracts the top professionals across the entire value-added chain of the above-mentioned sectors. You can meet with prominent manufacturers, suppliers, and industry peers to expand your network.

The Clean Show offers an incredible platform to network, learn, and explore the latest technologies in the industry. It features live product demos of cutting-edge machines and equipment. This trade show features several educational sessions by industry experts. Explore the latest innovations and gain valuable knowledge to optimize your cleaning services.

2. Surf Expo 2025

Dates 04 - 06 September 2025
Venue Orange County Convention Center, Orlando, FL, USA/td>
Focus Watersports and related products

Surf Expo is one of the most anticipated beach resort lifestyle and sports trade show events in the United States. It has the record of running for the longest period in its industry. It features surf, paddle, skate, and shoreline hard goods, apparel, and accessories. You can also explore a wide range of products related to resort, swim, boutique, footwear, gift & decor, and souvenir items.

Along with showcasing apparel and goods, this expo also features a series of fashion shows, award ceremonies, and much more. If you deal in watersports and coastal lifestyle solutions and are looking for promising opportunities to grow your brand and business, you should participate in the upcoming edition of this expo in Orlando.

3. ASI Show Orlando 2026

Dates 05 - 07 January 2026
Venue Orange County Convention Center, Orlando, FL
Focus Promotional products

The ASI Show (Advertising Specialty Institute Show) Orlando is considered one of the largest trade shows for the promotional products and advertising industry. It offers a distinct opportunity to showcase thousands of product ideas to an interested audience. It is a meeting platform for suppliers and clients to build relationships.

This show features a diverse educational program on highly relevant topics to improve your sales and marketing skills. Connect with peers through the networking activities at this event to share ideas, have fun together, and make lasting connections.

4. GCSAA Conference & Trade Show

Dates 31 January - 05 February 2026
Venue Orange County Convention Center, Orlando, FL
Focus Golf course and facility management

The Golf Course Superintendents Association of America (GCSAA) Conference and Trade Show is the premier event of the golf industry. It brings course owners and golf enthusiasts across the nation together on one platform. It is a wonderful opportunity to learn new methods, the latest trends, and network with others to forge lasting relationships.

Nearly 600 exhibitors across the golf course and facility management industry participate in this show. Participate in this event to reach 6,000+ qualified buyers, promote brand awareness, and expand your golf business to new/global markets.

5. Global Pet Expo 2026

Dates 25 - 27 March 2026
Venue Orange County Convention Center, Orlando, FL
Focus Pet product manufacturers, retailers, and pet professionals & owners

Global Pet Expo is the right trade show if you are looking for the latest pet products. It attracts 1,200+ exhibiting companies and features 3,500+ booths to showcase everything related to pets, from food to grooming items, including 1,000+ new products.

As the industry’s premier event featuring the newest, most innovative pet products, this pet expo attracts 20,000+ pet industry professionals. Exhibit here to connect with pet product manufacturers and retailers to expand your business network.

6. MRO Americas 2026

Dates 21 - 23 April 2026
Venue Orange County Convention Center, Orlando, FL
Focus Commercial aviation maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO)

Aviation Week Network’s MRO Americas is a distinguished event for the aviation maintenance community. It is dedicated to maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) in the commercial aviation sector. This show brings suppliers from all levels of the airline supply chain together under one roof. This unique event features interactive sessions led by 45+ senior-level speakers from prestigious airlines, OEMs, regulators, suppliers, lessors, and service providers.

You can learn about market insights, proprietary data, future opportunities and risks, trends, fleet data, transactions, life cycles, results-driven marketing services, and advertising at this aviation maintenance show. It also features the Military Pavilion, Aerospace Maintenance Competition, and Go Live! Theater, and A-WING jobfAIR.

7. Premiere Orlando Beauty Show 2026

Dates 30 May - 01 June 2026
Venue Orange County Convention Center – West Complex, Orlando, FL, USA
Focus Beauty products and professionals

Premiere Orlando is one of the largest beauty shows in the U.S. It connects beauty professionals from around the world to share the latest trends in the industry. As the industry is constantly changing, new technologies are emerging almost daily. This show highlights the newest innovations and techniques in the beauty sector.

Connect with industry experts to learn about the latest trends and build relationships with peers to establish lasting connections. Use these insights to enhance the efficiency of your business processes.

Effective Tips for Impactful Trade Show Campaigning in Orlando

Plan Early: Secure your booth space and logistics well in advance to avoid peak-season delays and charges.

Choose the Right Venue: Most major events in Orlando happen at the Orange County Convention Center; know the layout.

Leverage Local Services: Hire local booth builders for trade show booth rental services to cut down on transportation costs.

Customise Your Booth: Use creative design and branded visuals to stand out in a competitive environment.

Engage Visitors: Offer demos, samples, and incorporate interactive tech in your custom trade show displays to hold attention.
