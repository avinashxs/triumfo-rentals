Top 5 Trade Shows in Indianapolis 2025-2026

Every year, Indianapolis attracts business owners seeking to expand their companies in the best possible way. This Indiana city hosts a variety of trade shows that offer a great platform for participants to accomplish their business goals. If you are one of such business owners or decision-makers, looking for promising opportunities through trade shows in Indianapolis, you are at the right place.

In this blog, we are sharing a list of top trade shows that can help you reach your objectives. To make your brand stand out on the show floor, you must get an apt trade show exhibit rental in Indianapolis. This city attracts exhibitors across the globe; you need to be at your best with your booth.

Let’s explore the top 5 exhibitions to be held in Indianapolis during the upcoming days!

1. WWETT Show 2026

Dates 16 - 19 February 2026
Location Indiana Convention Center, Indianapolis, IN, USA
Focus Water and wastewater systems

The Water & Wastewater Equipment, Treatment & Transport Show is a highly anticipated event for water and wastewater service providers. It attracts 13,500+ professionals as visitors, 500+ leading suppliers as exhibitors, and features products and innovations that have applications in 20+ industries. It also features an education program that has a comprehensive lineup of sessions.

At this trade show, you get to showcase your water and wastewater treatment products to a highly interested audience, including decision-makers with purchasing power. You can boost your brand visibility among visitors from across 55+ countries. Put your business on the fast track to success by participating in the WWETT Show.

2. Indianapolis Boat, Sport and Travel Show 2026

Date 20 - 22 February & 25 February - 01 March 2026
Location Indiana State Fairgrounds, Indianapolis, IN, USA
Focus Boating and water sports

The Indianapolis Boat, Sport, and Travel Show is a unique trade fair that focuses on boats, boating, and water sports. It serves as a brilliant platform for dealers and manufacturers to showcase their boats and RVs to their core consumers across multiple markets. This event is full of opportunities and insights for fishing, hunting, camping, houseboat vacations, whitewater rafting, and so on.

By exhibiting at this show, you can connect with Midwesterners planning their vacations and looking to make boating-related equipment purchases. You can generate a great number of buyers/leads through this show. Participate and grab the opportunity to get ahead of your competition.

3. Craft Brewers and BrewExpo America 2026

Date 20 - 22 April 2026
Location Pennsylvania Convention Center, Philadelphia, PA, USA
Focus Beverage alcohol professionals

The Craft Brewers Conference & BrewExpo America is the premier gathering for brewing professionals. It attracts industry leaders, craft brewers, and suppliers from around the world. This dynamic event offers unparalleled opportunities for networking, education, and business growth, featuring expert-led seminars, hands-on workshops, and discussions on the latest trends and innovations in brewing.

At Brew Expo America, you can explore a vast trade show floor showcasing cutting-edge equipment, ingredients, and services tailored to the craft brewing industry. Whether you are an experienced brewer or just starting, this event is the ultimate destination to connect, learn, and elevate your craft.

4. American Coatings Show 2026

Date 05 - 07 May 2026
Location Indiana Convention Center, Indianapolis, IN, USA
Focus Coatings and adhesive materials, and equipment

The American Coatings Show is the premier event for the coatings industry in North America. It showcases the latest innovations, technologies, and trends across the coatings industry. It attracts top professionals, manufacturers, and suppliers of the industry and brings them under one roof. You can explore a vast exhibition floor featuring cutting-edge products, attend insightful technical presentations, and connect with industry leaders from around the world.

Designed for networking and knowledge-sharing, this event offers a unique opportunity to gain expertise in coatings technology, raw materials, and application techniques. Whether you are looking for business partnerships or staying ahead of industry advancements, this show is the ultimate destination for professionals in the coatings sector.

5. Indiana Comic & Pop Culture Convention 2026

Date 05 - 07 June 2026
Venue Indiana Convention Center, Indianapolis, IN, USA
Focus Coatings and adhesive materials, and equipment

The Indiana Comic Convention is a premier event for entertainment and media professionals. It attracts comics and pop culture enthusiasts from different parts of the world. This convention showcases a wide range of products and merchandise related to comic books, magazines, toys, games, anime, manga, Star Wars, Star Trek, artwork, sketches, and apparel.

This comic convention is quite popular among pop-culture enthusiasts because they get to meet creators, experts, and each other. Thus, it witnesses attendance of 45,000+ fans. This event features many fun activities for comic book fans, such as cosplay, gaming, cosplay contests, fan and cosplay meetups, and so on.

Effective ways to maximize your trade show ROI in Indianapolis

Stand Out Visually: Use bold design, signage, and engaging visuals in your trade show exhibit booth to make it stand out.

Engage Actively: Train your staff to smile, greet, and start conversations. Don't let them wait for visitors.

Offer Giveaways: You can attract traffic to your booth and boost recall by offering branded freebies.

Showcase Live Demos: Interactive demonstrations draw crowds and prove product value.

Leverage Social Media: Post updates, tag the event, and use hashtags. It will help you generate great ROI even with a 10x20 booth.
