Top 5 Security & Defense Trade Shows in the USA

Security and defense are dynamic and one of the most important sectors of modern times. This industry witnesses tremendous advancements on an almost daily basis. If your business belongs to the security and defense sector and you want to get to the forefront of this sector, participating in the relevant trade shows is what you need.

You can meet new potential customers and collaborators and gain valuable insights about the latest trends, practices, and technologies at the right trade show. Let us bring you the five most popular security and defense trade shows in the USA, compiled by renowned trade show exhibit booth builders.

1. AUSA Annual Meeting and Exposition 2025

Scheduled Date 13 - 15 Oct 2025
Location Walter E. Washington Convention Center, Washington DC, USA
Focus Development and welfare of the professional soldier

AUSA Annual Meeting and Exposition is one of the largest trade shows in the U.S. by attendance for the national security community. This exposition brings together industry, government, and military leaders to discuss the latest trends and technologies in defense. It attracts 33,000+ visitors from 80+ countries.

With over 700 exhibitors and 150 sessions planned, AUSA Meeting 2025 is a great opportunity to stay up-to-date on critical defense issues. Participating in this prestigious defense expo in the USA also connects you with industry partners, associations, and America’s Total Army supporters across the national, regional, and chapter levels.

2. American Society of Criminology (ASC) Annual Meeting 2025

Scheduled Date 12 - 15 Nov 2025
Location Marriott Marquis, Washington, DC, USA
Focus Criminology, law, and the democratic ideal

The American Society of Criminology Annual Meeting is a highly recognized security trade show in the USA. It attracts researchers and practitioners in criminology from around the world. This annual meeting is a one-stop shop for learning about the latest findings on crime and justice.

The ASC annual meeting offers a brilliant platform to learn about cutting-edge research in crime measurement, causes, prevention, control, and treatment. Along with engaging in in-depth discussions on pressing criminological issues, you can also network with leading professionals in this industry.

3. New York Custom Knife Show (NYCKS)

Scheduled Date 22 - 23 Aug 2025
Location The Westin Jersey City Newport, Jersey City, USA
Focus Knife and blade manufacturing and supply chain

New York Custom Knife Show, or NYCKS, is one of the most awaited trade shows for all knife enthusiasts. It is a premier event that showcases the finest blades and cutlery, including a wide array of custom knives, from everyday carry blades to heirloom-quality pieces. This is a must-attend event for collectors, knifemakers, and anyone seeking exceptional craftsmanship in knives and blades.

NYCKS also features educational seminars, product demonstrations, and opportunities to meet the talented makers behind the knives. Whether you are a seasoned manufacturer or supplier or an enthusiast just starting your collection, this trade show is the perfect platform to accomplish your knife-oriented goals.

4. SHOT Show

Scheduled Date 20 - 23 Jan 2026
Location Venetian Expo + Caesars Forum, Las Vegas, USA
Focus Shooting sports, hunting, law enforcement, and armed forces

SHOT Show is the biggest trade show for the shooting, hunting, outdoor, and law enforcement industries. This weapons expo brings together manufacturers, distributors, retailers, law enforcement personnel, and shooting sports enthusiasts from across the globe. It also features several educational seminars and demonstrations.

Organized by the National Shooting Sports Foundation (NSSF), this massive event showcases the latest firearms, ammunition, hunting gear, apparel, and accessories. You can explore innovative new products, showcased by suppliers and manufacturers of shooting, hunting, or outdoor arms. You can also expand your network by connecting with prominent industry professionals.

5. Modern Day Marine

Scheduled Date April 28 – April 30, 2026
Location Walter E. Washington Convention Center, Washington DC, USA
Focus Technologies and solutions for the USA’s expeditionary forces

Modern Day Marine is a highly anticipated military trade show for all Marines and military enthusiasts in the USA. This event showcases the latest advancements in Marine Corps equipment and technology, offering firsthand experience of the cutting edge of warfighting. Over 350 exhibitors participate and showcase everything from next-generation weaponry and communication systems to innovative unmanned vehicles and defense solutions. This military expo features interactive displays, product demonstrations, and presentations from leading military figures.

Modern Day Marine is a unique defence show that isn't just about hardware. It also encourages collaboration and knowledge exchange. It offers networking opportunities for Marines, industry leaders, and government officials.

How to Successfully Exhibit at a Military or Security Expo?

  • Know Your Audience: Identify if your visitors include military officials, procurement agents, or private security firms, and prepare tailored messaging.
  • Get Apt Stand Design: Your booth design should have a clean, military-grade aesthetic. Also, include private meeting areas for confidential discussions.
  • Compliance: Ensure compliance with U.S. Regulations such as ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations) and EAR (Export Administration Regulations).
  • Prioritize Security Protocols at Your Booth: Keep sensitive materials (brochures, specs, devices) locked or controlled, even if you have a 10x10 booth rental.
  • Showcase Authority Through Demonstration & Data: Highlight your product's tactical value, performance stats, and reliability in real-world conditions.
