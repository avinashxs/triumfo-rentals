Top 5 Environment & Waste Management Trade Shows in the USA

Environment and waste management have emerged as major concerns in modern times. There are lots of things happening in this regard almost every day. If you are a professional or associated with an organization dealing with environmental and waste management issues, participating in relevant trade shows and exhibitions can be a highly effective step to keep pace with the latest innovations and major developments in the industry.

Here are the 5 most distinguished environment and waste management-related trade show booths scheduled during the next few months. If you are looking for a trade show exhibit booth in the USA, these events provide excellent opportunities to showcase your solutions and connect with industry leaders.

1. Annual Tri-State Seminar

Scheduled Date August 4 - 7, 2025
Location South Point Hotel & Casino, Las Vegas, USA

The Annual Tri-State Seminar 2025 is an exclusive event dedicated to the experienced development of water, wastewater, and stormwater industry experts. Connecting industry leaders, technical experts, and specialists, this seminar offers a dynamic platform for education, networking, and hands-on training. Visitors will gain valuable insights through specialized workshops, technical sessions, and exhibits featuring the latest innovations and solutions.

Designed to enhance skills and knowledge, the event fosters collaboration among professionals from across the region. With a diverse lineup of speakers and exhibitors, the seminar provides an excellent opportunity to stay ahead in an ever-evolving industry.

2. The Water Expo

Scheduled Date August 20 - 21, 2025
Location Broward County Convention Center, Florida, USA

The Water Expo 2025 is the leading event for those proficient in the water, wastewater, sanitation, and environmental industries. Associating the top suppliers, experts, and decision-makers, this dynamic trade show offers the latest innovations, equipment, and solutions for water management. Guests can explore cutting-edge products, attend educational sessions, and network with industry leaders to stay ahead in this rapidly evolving field.

Developed for business growth and collaboration, the Water Expo provides a unique platform for manufacturers, distributors, and service providers to connect with buyers from the U.S., Latin America, and the Caribbean. Do not miss this opportunity to expand your reach!

3. Paper and Plastics Recycling Conference (PPRC)

Scheduled Date October 15 - 16, 2025
Location Hyatt Regency Chicago, Chicago, IL, USA

The Paper & Plastics Recycling Conference 2025 is a primary event aligning industry leaders, recyclers, and innovators to discuss the latest trends, challenges, and opportunities in the recycling sector. This conference serves as a key platform for networking, knowledge-sharing, and exploring cutting-edge solutions that drive sustainability and efficiency in paper and plastics recycling. Participants can expect insightful panel discussions, expert presentations, and valuable business connections.

With a focus on market developments, policy updates, and technological advancements, the event provides actionable insights for businesses navigating the evolving recycling landscape. Whether you are a recycler, processor, or industry stakeholder, this conference is a must-attend for staying ahead in the field.

4. Water & Wastewater Equipment, Treatment & Transport (WWETT)

Scheduled Date February 16 - 19, 2026
Location Indiana Convention Center, Indianapolis, IN, USA

The Water & Wastewater Equipment, Treatment & Transport (WWETT) Show is the top event for professionals in the wastewater and environmental services industry. Collaboratively getting in industry leaders, innovators, and experts, the event showcases cutting-edge equipment, advanced treatment technologies, and efficient transport solutions. Attendees can explore a vast exhibit hall featuring top suppliers, attend educational sessions led by industry experts, and network with peers from around the world.

With a strong focus on innovation and sustainability, the WWETT Show provides a platform for businesses to discover new solutions, enhance their operations, and stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry.

5. Waste Management Conference

Scheduled Date March 8 - 12, 2026
Location Phoenix Convention Center, Phoenix, AZ, USA

The Waste Management Conference 2026 is the premier international gathering for experts in radioactive waste management, decommissioning, and related fields. Bringing together industry leaders, researchers, and policymakers, the event fosters collaboration, innovation, and the exchange of cutting-edge solutions for sustainable waste practices. Attendees will have the opportunity to explore the latest advancements, regulatory updates, and best practices shaping the future of waste management.

With a diverse program featuring technical sessions, panel discussions, and an expansive exhibition, the conference provides a dynamic platform for networking and knowledge sharing. Participants can connect with experts, discover new technologies, and gain valuable insights into the evolving landscape of waste management.
