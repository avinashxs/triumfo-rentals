Why Choose a 10x20 Booth?

A 10x20 booth offers double the space of a standard 10x10, giving you more room for engagement, branding, product demos, and lead capture. With a professional 10x20 booth layout, you can create separate zones for meetings, product displays, and interactive activities—all without overwhelming your audience.
